# How to Pilot — Colorless Lugia (2023)

_Format: Scarlet & Violet-on Standard 2023 (post-rotation; Paldea Evolved-era Special
Energy). "Lugia VSTAR / Archeops" — the Colorless toolbox that dominated the previous
format and adapted to SV with new Special Energy._

## One-line identity
A Special-Energy acceleration toolbox: **Lugia VSTAR's Summoning Star** puts **2 Archeops
from your discard onto your bench**, and each **Archeops's Primal Turbo** attaches **2 Special
Energy from deck per turn** — up to **4 a turn**. That fuels a rotating cast of hard-hitting
Colorless attackers (Lugia itself, Snorlax, Archeops, Drapion V) that trade prizes favorably
against anything.

## The meta it lived in
Lugia VSTAR was the most powerful deck of the pre-SV format — the **BDIF** that at one point
put 21 of the Top 32 at a major on the deck — and it stayed strong post-rotation.
The SV rotation cost it Powerful Colorless Energy and Quick Ball, but Paldea Evolved handed it
a suite of **Colorless Special Energy** (Jet, V Guard, Gift, Therapeutic) and **Snorlax** as a
new attacker. Its edge is **flexibility + prize trade**: Summoning Star + Primal Turbo can
power *any* Colorless attacker for the matchup, and single-prize options keep the trade
favorable.

## Engine cards (why they're here)
- **Lugia V (138/195) ×4 → Lugia VSTAR (139/195) ×3** — the core. **Summoning Star** (VSTAR
  Power, once per game): put **2 Colorless non-Rule-Box Pokémon from discard onto the bench** —
  you use it to drop **2 Archeops**. **Tempest Dive**: 220 + discard a Stadium (answers Path to
  the Peak/Temple of Sinnoh). **Lugia V's Read the Wind** (discard 1 → draw 3) helps pitch
  Archeops early.
- **Archeops (147/195) ×4** — the engine. **Primal Turbo** (Ability): once per turn, search **2
  Special Energy from deck** and attach them to 1 Pokémon. Two Archeops = **4 Special Energy a
  turn** on top of your manual attach. It can *only* enter play via Summoning Star (no Fossil
  line), so get it into the discard early.
- **Lumineon V (40/172) ×2** — **Luminous Sign**: search a Supporter on entry — grabs
  Research/Boss/Iono; also a way to find your setup piece and a single-prize attacker.
- **Snorlax (143/196) ×2** — a bulky Colorless attacker; its high-Energy attack is trivial to
  power with Primal Turbo, and it's a resilient body.

## Support / tech (why the odd 1-ofs)
- **Drapion V (118/196) ×1** — a **Mew VMAX counter**: its attack punishes Fusion Strike/
  specific decks; a tech attacker Summoning Star can even recur.
- **Wyrdeer V (134/189) ×1** — its **Ability** (draw) / a Colorless attacker that consolidates
  Special Energy into one big hit.
- **Slaking V (58/78) ×1** — a big Colorless hitter (Primal Turbo powers its cost) for a clean
  KO.
- **Radiant Tsareena (16/195) ×1** — **Ability** heals your Pokémon (deny 2HKOs / grind).
- **Pumpkaboo (76/203) ×1** — **Pumpkin Pit** discards a Stadium (answer Path to the Peak/Temple
  of Sinnoh without needing to attack).
- **Professor's Research ×3 / Iono ×3 / Boss's Orders ×3 / Professor Burnet ×1** — draw + gust +
  disruption. **Research** (draw 7, discard hand — pitches Archeops); **Iono** (both players
  shuffle-and-draw to prize count — disruption); **Burnet** finds Pokémon + a Basic Energy.
- **Ultra Ball ×4 / Capturing Aroma ×4 / Nest Ball ×2** — search + discard (Ultra Ball pitches
  Archeops; Capturing Aroma finds a Pokémon + a Special Energy).
- **Choice Belt ×2** — +30 vs. V/VSTAR (Tempest Dive to 250, hits KO numbers).
- **Collapsed Stadium ×2 / Path to the Peak ×2** — your Stadiums: shrink benches / shut off
  opposing Abilities (and win the Stadium war so opposing Path doesn't lock *your* VSTAR Power
  before Summoning Star).
- **Special Energy suite: 4 Jet + 4 Double Turbo + 2 V Guard + 2 Gift + 2 Therapeutic** —
  **Jet Energy** (Colorless + free-retreat pivot), **Double Turbo** (2 Colorless, cheap — but
  −20 damage), **V Guard** (−30 from V attacks — tanks Lugia), **Gift** (Colorless + draw 2
  when attached), **Therapeutic** (Colorless + heal/status). All accelerated by Primal Turbo.

## Ideal early game
- **Turn 1:** get a **Lugia V** down (Ultra Ball/Capturing Aroma) and **discard 2 Archeops**
  (Research / Read the Wind / Ultra Ball). Bench setup pieces.
- **Turn 2:** **Lugia VSTAR → Summoning Star** for 2 Archeops → next turn **Primal Turbo ×2** =
  4 Special Energy → start attacking (Tempest Dive 220, or power a Snorlax/Slaking).

## The core loop
Discard 2 Archeops → **Summoning Star** them onto the bench → **Primal Turbo ×2** loads 4
Special Energy a turn onto whichever attacker the matchup wants → swing (Lugia Tempest Dive,
Snorlax, Slaking, Drapion V) → **Boss's Orders** for the KO, **Iono** to disrupt, **Choice
Belt** to reach numbers. You trade prizes favorably with a flexible attacker suite the
opponent can't fully prepare for.

## Priorities / rules of thumb
- **Two Archeops in the discard before Summoning Star** — a lone Archeops is fragile and half
  the engine; it's usually worth waiting a turn to have both.
- **Discard Archeops aggressively** — Ultra Ball/Research/Read the Wind exist to get them into
  the discard turn 1–2; that's the setup priority.
- **Pick the attacker for the matchup** — Tempest Dive (removes their Stadium), Snorlax/Slaking
  (big KO), Drapion V (vs. Mew) — the toolbox is the point.
- **Protect Summoning Star from Path + Marnie/Iono** — the classic counter locks your VSTAR
  Power turn 2; sequence around it (Pumpkaboo/your own Stadium).

## Weaknesses / bad matchups
- **Lightning (Lugia is Lightning-Weak)** — Miraidon/Lightning attackers hit Lugia for
  Weakness.
- **Path to the Peak + Iono/Marnie** — denying Summoning Star turn 2 sets you back badly.
- **Losing the Archeops** — if both Archeops get KO'd (or you only ever get one down), the
  engine collapses.
- **Temple of Sinnoh** — turns your Special Energy into nothing (no acceleration effect);
  Tempest Dive/Pumpkaboo answer it.

## Quick reference — the numbers
- Summoning Star (VSTAR Power): **2 Colorless non-Rule-Box Pokémon from discard → bench** (once
  per game).
- Primal Turbo (Archeops): **2 Special Energy from deck → 1 Pokémon** per turn (×2 Archeops = 4).
- Tempest Dive: **220 + discard a Stadium** (250 w/ Choice Belt).
- Read the Wind (Lugia V): discard 1 → **draw 3** (pitch Archeops).
