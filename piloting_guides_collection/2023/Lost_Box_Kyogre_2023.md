# How to Pilot — Lost Box Kyogre (2023)

_Format: Scarlet & Violet-on Standard 2023 (early SV, Lost Zone era). This build mirrors
Shao Tong Yen's 2023 Junior World Championship-**winning** "Lost Box Kyogre" deck._

## One-line identity
A single-prize Lost Zone toolbox with a game-ending combo: the **Comfey + Colress's
Experiment** engine banks cards in the **Lost Zone** to unlock tiered payoffs (4 = Cramorant,
7 = Mirage Gate Energy acceleration, 10 = Sableye's Lost Mine spread), and once your deck is
nearly empty you fire **Kyogre's Aqua Storm** to take **up to four Prizes in one turn**. Fast,
flexible, and hard to race.

## The meta it lived in
2023 Standard (up to Obsidian Flames) was wide-open — Gardevoir ex, Lugia VSTAR, Mew VMAX,
Giratina VSTAR, Miraidon ex, and Lost Zone Box were all viable. Lost Box thrived because its
**single-prize attackers trade favorably** against the multi-prize ex/V decks, and the Kyogre
finish punishes boards full of two-prize Pokémon. Shao Tong Yen won the Junior World
Championship in Yokohama with this exact 60.

## The Lost Zone thresholds (the whole framework)
Cards go to the **Lost Zone** (unrecoverable) to unlock powers at set counts:
- **4 in the Lost Zone → Cramorant's Spit Innocently** (110, free) — an early attacker.
- **7 → Mirage Gate** (attach 2 Energy of any type from deck to 1 Pokémon) — your acceleration.
- **10 → Sableye's Lost Mine** (place 12 damage counters spread) — a multi-KO spread attack.
Your whole game is **racing to these numbers** with Comfey/Colress's Experiment.

## Engine cards (why they're here)
- **Comfey (79/196) ×4** — **Flower Selecting** (Ability): look at top 2, put 1 in hand and 1
  in the **Lost Zone** — the engine that draws *and* banks Lost Zone cards each turn (free
  retreat, so you loop it). The heart of the deck.
- **Colress's Experiment ×4** — look at top 5, take 3, put **2 in the Lost Zone** — the fastest
  Lost Zone accelerator; play it *before* Comfey for max information.
- **Sableye (70/196) ×2** — **Lost Mine** (needs 10 in Lost Zone): place 12 damage counters
  however you like across the opponent's board — takes multiple KOs on small targets (Comfey,
  Kirlia) at once.
- **Kyogre (3/25) ×1** — the finisher. **Aqua Storm**: needs a big Energy investment (Mirage
  Gate + Energy Recycler + Super Rod loop), does damage to multiple Benched Pokémon — set up
  perfectly it **takes 4 Prizes in one turn** to close the game.

## Support / tech (why the odd 1-ofs)
- **Cramorant (50/196) ×1** — **Spit Innocently** (110, free at 4 Lost Zone) — the turn-1/
  early attacker to grab a prize or snipe.
- **Radiant Greninja (46/189) ×1** — **Concealed Cards** (discard Energy → draw 2) draw/cycle,
  and **Moonlight Shuriken** (discard 2 Water → 90 to two benched) as a snipe attacker.
- **Dragonite V (SWSH154) ×1** — **Dragon Gale**: 280 (unconditional big damage) with 30 to
  your own bench — the "just KO it" attacker (needs Mirage Gate Energy).
- **Raikou V (48/172) ×1** — **Fleet-Footed** (draw a card when it's your Active on turn 1) /
  **Lightning Rondo** — a Lightning attacker + Forest Seal Stone holder.
- **Manaphy (41/172) ×1** — **Wave Veil**: protect your bench from snipe (mirror / Radiant
  Greninja) — get it down early vs. spread decks.
- **Klara ×1** — recover 2 Pokémon + 2 Energy (or the split) from discard — a recovery
  Supporter for the long game.
- **Battle VIP Pass ×4** — turn-1 only: bench 2 Basics (Comfey + an attacker) — the ideal
  explosive opener.
- **Switch Cart ×4 / Escape Rope ×4** — free retreat / pivot to keep looping Comfey and dodge
  gusts (Escape Rope also disrupts).
- **Mirage Gate ×4** — the 7-Lost-Zone Energy accelerator (2 Energy of any type from deck) —
  powers Dragonite/Kyogre/Greninja.
- **Nest Ball ×3 / Hisuian Heavy Ball ×1 / Echoing Horn ×1** — search Basics (Heavy Ball grabs
  your many singletons; Echoing Horn recovers a KO'd Basic).
- **Energy Recycler ×2 / Super Rod ×2 / Pal Pad ×1** — recycle Energy/Pokémon (fuel the Kyogre
  combo) and recover Supporters (chain Colress's Experiment).
- **Forest Seal Stone ×2** — Tool: a **once-per-game "search any card"** VSTAR-Power-style
  effect on a Pokémon — grabs the exact combo piece.
- **PokéStop ×2** — Stadium: flip 3 cards, take Items, discard the rest — digs for Items (and
  can seed Energy for recycling).
- **5 Water + 3 Psychic + 3 Lightning** — spread typing for Mirage Gate flexibility (Kyogre/
  Greninja Water, Raikou Lightning, etc.).

## Ideal early game
- **Turn 1:** **Battle VIP Pass** → Comfey + an attacker; **Colress's Experiment** then
  **Flower Selecting** to bank Lost Zone cards. Aim to reach **4** (Cramorant online) and set
  up.
- **Turn 2:** hit **7** (Mirage Gate) to accelerate onto Dragonite/Greninja and start taking
  prizes; keep looping Comfey toward **10** (Sableye).

## The core loop
Race the Lost Zone with **Colress's Experiment + Comfey** → attack with the right single-prize
piece (Cramorant early, Radiant Greninja/Dragonite mid, Sableye's Lost Mine for multi-KO) →
protect your bench with **Manaphy** → and when your deck is nearly empty, assemble the
**Kyogre Aqua Storm** 4-prize finish (Mirage Gate + Energy Recycler + Super Rod). You trade
single prizes favorably and close with the combo.

## Priorities / rules of thumb
- **Colress's Experiment before Comfey** — see 5 cards first so Flower Selecting banks the
  right card.
- **Know your thresholds** — 4/7/10 gate your whole game; count the Lost Zone every turn and
  plan which payoff you're racing to.
- **Manaphy down vs. spread** — an unprotected bench of Comfey dies to Lost Mine / Moonlight
  Shuriken in the mirror.
- **Set up Kyogre only when you can take 4** — the combo needs the Energy loop; don't fire it
  half-baked.

## Weaknesses / bad matchups
- **Hisuian Goodra VSTAR / tanky single-attacker decks** — hard to KO and won't give you easy
  prizes; a grind (Echoing Horn helps).
- **Bench snipe against your Comfey** — without Manaphy, the mirror / Greninja picks apart your
  engine.
- **Getting stuck below a threshold** — a clunky start that can't reach 4/7 leaves you passing
  turns.
- **Aggressive ex decks that race the setup** — if they OHKO faster than you set up, the
  single-prize plan can fall behind.

## Quick reference — the numbers
- Lost Zone thresholds: **4 = Cramorant, 7 = Mirage Gate, 10 = Sableye Lost Mine**.
- Flower Selecting (Comfey): top 2 → 1 hand, **1 Lost Zone** (Ability, free retreat).
- Colress's Experiment: top 5 → take 3, **2 to Lost Zone**.
- Kyogre Aqua Storm: the **4-prize** finisher (needs the Energy loop).
