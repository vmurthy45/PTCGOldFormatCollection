# How to Pilot — Gardevoir (2023)

_Format: Scarlet & Violet-on Standard 2023 (SV base-on). "Gardevoir ex" — the standout deck
of the SV era's opening, widely rated the best deck in the format at EUIC 2023._

## One-line identity
A Psychic engine with near-unlimited Energy: **Gardevoir ex's Psychic Embrace** attaches **any
number of basic Psychic Energy from your discard** to your Psychic Pokémon each turn (2 damage
counters per attach as the cost), fueling a rotating cast of attackers — **Gardevoir ex's
Miraculous Magic**, **Zacian V**, **Cresselia**, and single-prize hitters — while **Kirlia's
Refinement** draws you through the deck. Consistent, flexible, OHKO-capable.

## The meta it lived in
Gardevoir ex was the clear standout when Scarlet & Violet landed — an **autonomous draw engine
(Kirlia), massive Energy acceleration (Psychic Embrace), and OHKO power** in one Stage-2 line.
It was the best deck at EUIC 2023 and stayed top-tier all year. Its main trouble came from
**Lost Zone Sableye** decks (single-prize aggro that attacks first) and **Path to the Peak**
(it's a Rule-Box deck). It beats most multi-prize decks (Mew, Miraidon, Goodra) once set up.

## Engine cards (why they're here)
- **Ralts (60/189 ×3 + 67/195 ×1) → Kirlia (68/195 ×3 + 60/198 ×1) → Gardevoir ex (86/198) ×2
  + Gardevoir (61/198) ×2** — the core. **Psychic Embrace** (Gardevoir ex Ability): attach any
  number of basic Psychic Energy **from the discard** to your Psychic Pokémon (2 damage
  counters per Energy) — effectively unlimited acceleration. **Kirlia's Refinement** (Ability):
  discard a card → draw 2 — the autonomous draw engine (why you run a fat Kirlia count). The
  non-ex **Gardevoir (61/198)** adds **Shining Arcana** (a search/setup Ability) — a key
  consistency piece.
- **Zacian V (16/25) ×1** — **Roar of the Sword** (search a Trainer) + **Intrepid Slash**: a
  hard-hitting attacker Psychic Embrace can fully load for a big clean KO (and it only gives up
  2 prizes).
- **Cresselia (74/196) ×1** — **Full Moon** loads Energy / **its attack** heals + evens the
  prize trade — an efficient attacker that also moves damage (great when you're behind).
- **Radiant Greninja (46/189) ×1** — **Concealed Cards** draw/cycle + **Moonlight Shuriken**
  snipe; extra consistency and a spread option.

## Support / tech (why the odd 1-ofs)
- **Mew (11/25) ×1** — a bench-sitter / utility (protection or a small attacker depending on
  build).
- **Manaphy (41/172) ×1** — **Wave Veil**: protect your bench from snipe (Lost Zone Greninja/
  Sableye, mirror).
- **Lumineon V (40/172) ×1** — **Luminous Sign**: search a Supporter on entry (Boss/Iono/
  Research) — turn-1 consistency.
- **Iono ×3 / Professor's Research ×2 / Boss's Orders ×2** — draw + gust + disruption. **Iono**
  (shuffle-draw to prize count) disrupts *and* refills; **Boss's Orders** pulls the KO.
- **Battle VIP Pass ×4** — turn-1 only: bench 2 Basics (Ralts + a support) — the ideal opener.
- **Level Ball ×4 / Ultra Ball ×3 / Fog Crystal ×2 / Rare Candy ×3** — search: **Level Ball**
  (Ralts/Kirlia), **Fog Crystal** (a Psychic Pokémon *or* Psychic Energy — fuels Embrace),
  **Rare Candy** (jump Ralts → Gardevoir ex a turn early).
- **Super Rod ×2 / Pal Pad ×1 / Lost Vacuum ×1** — recover Pokémon/Energy (recycle Psychic for
  Embrace), recover Supporters, and remove a Tool/Stadium.
- **Forest Seal Stone ×1** — once-per-game "search any card" on a Pokémon — grabs the exact
  piece.
- **Artazon ×1 / Collapsed Stadium ×1** — Stadiums: **Artazon** (search a non-Rule-Box Basic —
  more Ralts/support) and **Collapsed Stadium** (shrink benches; also removes your own damaged
  Pokémon to deny prizes), plus a counter to opposing Path to the Peak.
- **10 Psychic + 2 Reversal Energy** — a high basic Psychic count is the fuel for Psychic
  Embrace; **Reversal Energy** powers a single-prize attacker for a big comeback hit when
  you're behind on prizes.

## Ideal early game
- **Turn 1:** **Battle VIP Pass** → Ralts + a support; start evolving. Get **Psychic into the
  discard** (Research/Fog Crystal) so Embrace has fuel.
- **Turn 2:** **Rare Candy → Gardevoir ex**, start **Kirlia Refinement** draw, and use **Psychic
  Embrace** to load an attacker (Zacian/Cresselia/Gardevoir) for the KO.

## The core loop
Draw through the deck with **Kirlia Refinement** → **Psychic Embrace** to pour discarded
Psychic Energy onto your chosen attacker → swing for KOs (Gardevoir ex, a loaded Zacian V, or
a Reversal-Energy single-prizer when behind) → **Cresselia** to even the prize trade / move
damage, **Boss's Orders** for the target, **Iono** to disrupt. Recycle Psychic with Super Rod
so Embrace never runs dry.

## Priorities / rules of thumb
- **Seed the discard with Psychic** — Embrace pulls from there; Research/Fog Crystal/Ultra Ball
  fill it. No discarded Psychic = no acceleration.
- **Mind the 2-counters cost** — Psychic Embrace damages the Pokémon you load; don't over-attach
  onto a Pokémon you need to survive (or use it on an attacker you're about to swing with).
- **Kirlia is the engine — run/protect it** — Refinement is your draw; a wide Kirlia bench keeps
  you flowing.
- **Answer Path to the Peak** — you're a Rule-Box deck; win the Stadium war (Artazon/Collapsed)
  or you lose your Abilities.

## Weaknesses / bad matchups
- **Darkness / Metal (Gardevoir ex is Metal-Weak)** — and Lost Zone Sableye's single-prize aggro
  attacks first and races you — the toughest matchup.
- **Path to the Peak** — shuts off Psychic Embrace + Refinement (your whole engine); you must
  bounce it.
- **Fast single-prize aggro** — anything that takes the early prize lead before you set up
  (Collapsed Stadium/Cresselia/Roxanne help you claw back).
- **Snipe on Kirlia/Ralts** — killing the engine before it evolves sets you back (Manaphy only
  helps vs. bench spread, not gusts).

## Quick reference — the numbers
- Psychic Embrace: attach **any number of basic Psychic from discard** to Psychic Pokémon (2
  damage counters each).
- Kirlia Refinement: **discard 1 → draw 2** (Ability, per Kirlia, per turn).
- Rare Candy: jump **Ralts → Gardevoir ex**; Fog Crystal: **a Psychic Pokémon or Psychic
  Energy**.
- Reversal Energy: powers a **single-prize** attacker for a comeback KO.
