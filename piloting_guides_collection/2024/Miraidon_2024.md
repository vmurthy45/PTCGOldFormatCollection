# How to Pilot — Miraidon (2024)

_Format: Scarlet & Violet Standard 2024. "Miraidon ex" — this build mirrors Jesse Parker's
2024 Masters World Championship **Semifinalist** list; a fast, consistent Lightning toolbox._

## One-line identity
The turbo consistency deck: **Miraidon ex's Tandem Unit** searches **2 Basic Lightning
Pokémon straight to your bench** (usable by every Miraidon, at any time), and **Electric
Generator** accelerates Lightning Energy from your deck — so you flood the board and attack
by turn 1–2 with a **toolbox of Basic attackers** (Miraidon's 220 Photon Blaster, Iron Hands'
extra-prize Amp You Very Much, Bloodmoon Ursaluna's 240). Fast, flexible, and hard to disrupt.

## The meta it lived in
2024's wide field rewarded consistency, and Miraidon has one of the best engines in the game:
Tandem Unit turns a single Nest Ball into a full board, so the deck almost never bricks. It's a
proactive, hard-hitting deck that races anything and swings the prize trade with **Iron Hands
ex**. Jesse Parker took it to the Masters Semifinals at Worlds 2024. Its main predators are
**Iron Thorns** (shuts off Tandem Unit) and Fighting-Weakness hits.

## Engine cards (why they're here)
- **Miraidon ex (81/198) ×2** — the core. **Tandem Unit** (Ability): search **2 Basic Lightning
  Pokémon → bench** — usable by *every* Miraidon, any time, no restriction; this is the engine
  that builds your whole board (one Nest Ball → a full bench). **Photon Blaster**: 220 (can't
  attack next turn — but a *second* Miraidon can). Only 2 copies because Tandem Unit finds the
  others.
- **Electric Generator ×4** — look at top 5, attach up to **2 Basic Lightning from there to your
  benched Lightning Pokémon** — the acceleration that powers a turn-2 (or turn-1 going second)
  attack. Thin your deck first (Tandem Unit/Ultra Ball) to hit them.
- **Iron Hands ex (70/182) ×2** — the prize-swinger. **Amp You Very Much**: 120 and, if it KOs,
  **take an extra Prize card** — turns a 2-prize KO into 3, or maps out a 3-3 prize plan.
  Expensive, but Electric Generator/Double Turbo powers it (100 with Double Turbo, still enough
  for small Basics).
- **Squawkabilly ex (169/193) ×2** — **Squawk and Seize**: discard hand, draw 6 (a turn-1
  optional-mulligan / Professor's Research) — the consistency starter that digs to your engine.

## The attacker toolbox (why the 1-ofs)
- **Raikou V (48/172) ×1** — **Lightning Rondo**: 20 + 20 per benched Pokémon (both sides) — a
  cheap turn-1-going-second attacker that often 1HKOs a Basic; **Fleet-Footed** draws.
- **Bloodmoon Ursaluna ex (141/167) ×1** — **Blood Moon**: 240 (Colorless — powered by Double
  Turbo); **Seasoned Skill** drops the cost by 1 per prize the opponent has taken — a late-game
  finisher (with **Defiance Band** it hits 270 to KO a Charizard ex).
- **Zapdos (29/78) ×1** — a Lightning attacker to hit exact numbers (230-HP targets / Mimikyu).
- **Iron Bundle (56/182) ×1** — **Hypnoblast**/its Ability forces the opponent to switch — drag
  their win-condition out of the Active spot to stall/disrupt.
- **Mew ex (151/165) ×1** — **Restart** (fill hand) — draw when your hand is small; free-retreat
  pivot.
- **Fezandipiti ex (38/64) ×1** — **Flip the Script**: draw 3 after a KO last turn — comeback
  draw.
- **Lumineon V (40/172) ×1** — **Luminous Sign**: search a Supporter on entry (Boss/Research).
- **Radiant Greninja (46/189) ×1** — **Concealed Cards** draw/cycle + a snipe attacker.

## Support / tech (why the counts)
- **Arven ×4** — search a Tool + an Item (Forest Seal Stone/Bravery Charm + Electric Generator) —
  the consistency glue.
- **Boss's Orders ×3 / Professor's Research ×1** — gust the KO target + draw.
- **Ultra Ball ×4 / Nest Ball ×4** — search (thin the deck for Electric Generator; find Miraidon).
- **Prime Catcher ×1 (ACE SPEC) / Switch Cart ×2** — guaranteed gust + switch; pivot (reset
  Photon Blaster / retreat a sleeping/loaded Pokémon).
- **Forest Seal Stone ×2** — once-per-game "search any card" on a Pokémon — grab the missing
  piece.
- **Bravery Charm ×1 / Rescue Board ×1 / Town Store ×1** — +HP (Iron Hands survives a hit),
  free retreat, and a Stadium/Tool search.
- **16 Lightning + 2 Double Turbo Energy** — a huge Lightning count so Electric Generator always
  hits; **Double Turbo** powers Iron Hands/Ursaluna's Colorless costs.

## Ideal early game
- **Going second (preferred):** **Miraidon ex → Tandem Unit** for 2 Lightning Pokémon →
  **Electric Generator** onto the bench → attack immediately (Raikou Lightning Rondo, or set up
  Iron Hands). **Squawkabilly** first if your hand is rough.
- **Going first:** Tandem Unit to flood the bench and charge with Electric Generator for a turn-2
  Photon Blaster.

## The core loop
**Tandem Unit** to build the board (chain it — find a Pokémon *and* another Miraidon, then
Tandem Unit again) → **Electric Generator** to accelerate → pick the attacker for the job:
**Photon Blaster (220)** for big KOs, **Amp You Very Much** for the extra-prize swing,
**Bloodmoon** as a finisher. **Boss's Orders/Prime Catcher** pick the target; a second Miraidon
resets the Photon Blaster drawback. You out-tempo and out-consistency the field.

## Priorities / rules of thumb
- **Chain Tandem Unit** — one Miraidon finds another + a Pokémon; do it again for a full board
  off almost nothing. This is the deck's consistency.
- **Thin before Electric Generator** — Tandem Unit/Ultra Ball remove Pokémon/cards so Generator
  hits its 2 Energy off the top 5.
- **Map the prizes with Iron Hands** — Amp You Very Much's extra prize sets up 3-3 or 3-2-1
  plans; sequence KOs so it lands.
- **Reset Photon Blaster** — attack with a *second* Miraidon (or another attacker) so you never
  skip a turn.

## Weaknesses / bad matchups
- **Iron Thorns ex** — **Initialization shuts off Tandem Unit** (and Mew); use Tandem Unit
  *before* they get Iron Thorns Active, and lean on Boss + non-Ability attackers.
- **Fighting-Weakness hits** — Miraidon/Iron Hands are Lightning (some take extra from Fighting/
  specific tech).
- **Mimikyu (Safeguard)** — immune to your ex attackers; Boss around it or use Zapdos/a non-ex.
- **Prized Miraidon** — you run only 2; a prized/late Miraidon slows the engine (Ultra/Nest
  mitigate).

## Quick reference — the numbers
- Tandem Unit: search **2 Basic Lightning Pokémon → bench** (every Miraidon, any time).
- Photon Blaster: **220** (can't attack next turn — a 2nd Miraidon can).
- Electric Generator: top 5 → attach **2 Basic Lightning to benched Lightning Pokémon**.
- Amp You Very Much (Iron Hands): **120 + take an extra Prize** on a KO; Bloodmoon: **240** (270
  w/ Defiance Band).
