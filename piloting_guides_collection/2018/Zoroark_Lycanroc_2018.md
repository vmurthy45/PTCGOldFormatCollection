# How to Pilot — Zoroark Lycanroc (2018)

_Format: Sun & Moon-on Standard 2018 (BKT–FLI). "ZoroRoc" — a Tier-1 (arguably the best)
Zoroark-GX variant of the year._

## One-line identity
The most flexible Zoroark deck: **Zoroark-GX's Trade** draws you through the deck, **Riotous
Beating** scales with your board, and **Lycanroc-GX's Bloodthirsty Eyes** drags up any
benched Pokémon (no Supporter) so you KO exactly what you want. Add **Mew-EX** and a baby
**Buzzwole** for coverage and you can take 6 prizes in ~3 attacks against most decks.

## The meta it lived in
ZoroRoc was considered by many the center of the 2018 metagame — oppressive against other
Zoroark variants and slow Stage-2 decks thanks to Bloodthirsty Eyes disruption. It pairs
Zoroark's raw consistency with Lycanroc's gust-on-evolve and a strong closing GX attack. Its
only shaky spots were dedicated OHKO decks (Rayquaza-GX/Vikavolt) and the Fighting-Weakness
that all Zoroark decks share.

## Engine cards (why they're here)
- **Zorua (52/73) ×4 → Zoroark-GX (53/73) ×4** — the core. **Trade** (discard 1 → draw 2),
  **Riotous Beating** (20 × your Pokémon), **Trickster-GX** (copy an attack). The best draw
  engine in the format.
- **Rockruff (75/131) ×3 → Lycanroc-GX (74/145) ×2** — **Bloodthirsty Eyes** (Ability):
  switch a benched opposing Pokémon Active on evolve — a free gust to line up KOs. **Claw
  Slash** (110, buffable to 180 with Choice Band/Kukui/Strong) and **Dangerous Rogue-GX**
  (scales with their bench) give real attacking power.
- **Tapu Lele-GX (60/145) ×3** — Wonder Tag: any Supporter (Brigette, Guzma, Cynthia, Mallow).
- **Puzzle of Time ×4** — retrieve any 2 cards (DCE, Guzma, a KO'd attacker) — the recursion
  engine.

## Support / tech (why the odd 1-ofs)
- **Oranguru (113/149) ×1** — benched draw/recycle engine that survives N (doesn't rely on
  Trade).
- **Mew-EX (XY126) ×1** — copies a benched attacker (a **Buzzwole counter** — copy Riotous
  Beating to 1HKO the Psychic-Weak Buzzwole).
- **Buzzwole (77/131) ×1** — a Fighting single-prize attacker tech: Jet Punch snipe / a body
  that trades well and helps the mirror.
- **Brigette ×4 / N ×3 / Cynthia ×2 / Guzma ×2 / Sycamore ×1 / Mallow ×1 / Kukui ×1 / Acerola
  ×1** — a rich toolbox Supporter suite (all tutorable via Tapu Lele). **Mallow** finds combo
  pieces; **Kukui** (+20) hits exact KO numbers; **Acerola** resets/denies prizes.
- **Ultra Ball ×4 / Evosoda ×2 / Field Blower ×2 / Enhanced Hammer ×1 / Multi Switch ×1** —
  search, Supporter-free evolves, Tool/Stadium removal, special-Energy strip, and a switch
  trick.
- **Choice Band ×1 / Float Stone ×2 / Parallel City ×1** — +30 vs. GX/EX, free retreat, bench/
  damage control.
- **4 DCE + 2 Strong Energy + 2 Fighting** — DCE powers Riotous Beating; Strong (+20) and
  Fighting fuel Lycanroc's Claw Slash and the Buzzwole tech to exact numbers.

## Ideal early game
- **Turn 1:** **Brigette** for Zorua ×2–3 + Rockruff; wide board, pass.
- **Turn 2:** evolve **Zoroark-GX**, **Trade** to dig, and either **Riotous Beating** or
  evolve **Lycanroc-GX** (Bloodthirsty Eyes) to gust + set up a KO.

## The core loop
Trade every turn for gas; use **Bloodthirsty Eyes / Guzma** to drag up the exact target;
KO it with **Riotous Beating**, a buffed **Claw Slash**, or **Dangerous Rogue-GX**. **Mew-EX**
answers Buzzwole; **Puzzle of Time** recurs your pieces; **N** disrupts. With Lycanroc's gust
you dismantle the opponent's plan and race to 6 prizes in ~3 big turns.

## Priorities / rules of thumb
- **Bloodthirsty Eyes wins games** — evolve Lycanroc at the moment that drags up a KO the
  opponent can't afford to lose; it's a Supporter-free gust, so you can still N/Cynthia.
- **Brigette turn 1** — the setup that powers everything.
- **Know your Claw Slash math** — Choice Band + Kukui + Strong turns 110 into a 1HKO on
  Zoroark/other GX.
- **Mew-EX vs. Buzzwole** — copy the right attack to 1HKO the Psychic-Weak Beasts.

## Weaknesses / bad matchups
- **Fighting (Buzzwole/Lycanroc, Buzz/Shrine)** — Zoroark-GX is Fighting-Weak; the shared
  Zoroark problem (your own Buzzwole/Mew-EX techs help).
- **Dedicated OHKO decks (Rayquaza-GX/Vikavolt, Ultra Necrozma)** — can out-damage your 2HKO
  grind; a shaky matchup.
- **Garbotoxin (opposing Garbodor)** — turns off Trade; Field Blower or race.
- **Shrine of Punishment** — chip damage on your GX-heavy board.

## Quick reference — the numbers
- Trade: **discard 1 → draw 2**; Riotous Beating: **20 × your Pokémon**.
- Bloodthirsty Eyes: **free gust on evolving Lycanroc-GX**.
- Claw Slash: **110** base → ~180 with Choice Band + Kukui + Strong; Dangerous Rogue-GX
  scales with their bench.
- Puzzle of Time (×2): **retrieve any 2 cards**.
