# How to Pilot — Banette Garbodor (2018)

_Format: Sun & Moon-on Standard 2018 (BKT–CES). "Garbanette" — this build mirrors Magnus
Pedersen's 2018 World Championship-**winning** (Senior Division) list._

## One-line identity
A disruptive single-Energy toolbox: **Banette-GX** attacks for just one Psychic
(**Shadow Chant** scales with Supporters in your discard) and its **Shady Move** Ability
slides a damage counter anywhere — even onto the opponent — for surprise KOs. Back it with
the classic **Garbodor** split (**Garbotoxin** lock / **Trashalanche** punish) and
**Wobbuffet** for a second Ability-lock, and you strangle the opponent's engine.

## The meta it lived in
Garbanette is the spiritual successor to Espeon-GX/Garbodor — but stronger, because
Banette-GX only ever needs one Energy and doesn't depend on drawing DCE. In the Zoroark-GX
era, its **Fighting Resistance** and Shady Move damage-shifting made it a strong answer to
Buzzwole/Shrine (the deck that preys on Zoroark), while Garbotoxin locked the mirror and
Malamar. Magnus Pedersen won the Senior Division World Championship with it.

## Engine cards (why they're here)
- **Shuppet (63/168) ×2 → Banette-GX (66/168) ×2** — the main attacker. **Shadow Chant**:
  30 + 10 per Supporter in your discard (up to +100) for a single Psychic — a cheap, scaling
  hit. **Shady Move** (Ability): once per turn, move a damage counter from one Pokémon to any
  other (including the opponent's) — sets up 1HKOs and finishes softened targets. **Tomb
  Hunt-GX** recovers 3 cards. Fighting-Resistant = great vs. Buzzwole.
- **Trubbish (56/122) ×4 → Garbodor** — the split line:
  - **Garbodor (57/122, "Garbotoxin") ×2** — with a Tool, all Abilities off.
  - **Garbodor (51/145, "Trashalanche") ×2** — 20 × the opponent's Items in the discard.
- **Wobbuffet (36/119) ×1** — **Bide Barricade**: while it's Active, Basic Pokémon Abilities
  are shut off — a second, Tool-free Ability-lock angle (and a body that stalls).
- **Tapu Lele-GX (60/145) ×2** — Wonder Tag: any Supporter (Brigette, N, Guzma).

## Support / tech (why the odd 1-ofs)
- **Drampa-GX (115/145) ×1** — **Berserk** (180) and **Righteous Edge** (chip + Energy
  removal); a Colorless heavy-hitter for the KOs Banette can't reach, and Righteous Edge
  strips Energy from Energy-reliant decks.
- **Buzzwole (77/131) ×1** — a Fighting single-prize attacker tech (mirror / specific
  matchups).
- **Jirachi (XY67) ×1** — **Stellar Guidance**: search a Supporter (extra consistency).
- **Sycamore ×4 / N ×4 / Guzma ×3 / Cynthia ×2 / Brigette ×1** — heavy control Supporter suite
  (and Shadow Chant *wants* Supporters in the discard, so playing them fuels your attack).
- **Ultra Ball ×4 / Mysterious Treasure ×1 / Field Blower ×2 / Rescue Stretcher ×1** —
  search, Tool/Stadium removal (toggle your own Garbotoxin), recovery.
- **Float Stone ×4 / Choice Band ×3 / Parallel City ×2** — free retreat (and Garbotoxin's
  Tool), +30 vs. GX/EX (Banette + Choice Band + full discard = 170 on a Tapu Lele-GX),
  and bench/damage control.
- **4 Psychic + 4 Rainbow + 3 DCE** — Psychic powers Banette/Shadow Chant; Rainbow enables
  Garbodor + Drampa; DCE for Drampa/off-costs.

## Ideal early game
- **Turn 1:** **Brigette** (via Tapu Lele) for Shuppet + Trubbish; bench your pieces. Pass.
- **Turn 2:** evolve **Banette-GX**, **Shadow Chant** for a cheap hit (already boosted if
  you've discarded Supporters), and start setting up **Garbotoxin**.

## The core loop
Attack cheaply every turn with **Shadow Chant** (which grows as your Supporter discard grows
— and you're playing Supporters anyway), use **Shady Move** to slide counters and snipe/
finish exact KOs, and lock the opponent's Abilities with **Garbotoxin** (or **Wobbuffet**).
Bring in **Drampa Berserk** for a big KO, hold **Trashalanche** vs. Item decks, and grind
the prize trade with Fighting-Resistant, single-Energy attackers.

## Priorities / rules of thumb
- **Shady Move sets up 1HKOs** — pre-place/relocate counters so Shadow Chant (or a bench
  snipe) finishes a Pokémon it otherwise couldn't.
- **Your Supporters fuel Shadow Chant** — the Supporter-heavy build is a feature; a fat
  Supporter discard = a bigger attack.
- **Garbotoxin / Wobbuffet vs. Ability decks** — pick your lock; Wobbuffet needs no Tool.
- **Don't over-bench GX** — Banette/Garbodor decks want to keep the prize trade tight; Tapu
  Lele-GX and Drampa are liabilities if left exposed (and to opposing Shrine).

## Weaknesses / bad matchups
- **Dark (Darkrai, opposing Zoroark's Dark attacks)** — Banette-GX is Dark-Weak (some lists
  add Weakness Policy for exactly this).
- **Field Blower decks** — removing your Garbotoxin Tool blunts the lock.
- **Fast 1HKO decks** — anything that races before Garbotoxin + Shady Move take over.
- **Item-light / Ability-light decks** — blunt both Trashalanche and Garbotoxin at once.

## Quick reference — the numbers
- Shadow Chant: **30 + 10 per Supporter in your discard** (max +100) for 1 Psychic.
- Shady Move: **move 1 damage counter** anywhere (Ability, once/turn).
- Garbotoxin: **all Abilities off** with a Tool; Wobbuffet: **Basic Abilities off** while
  Active.
- Banette + Choice Band + full discard = **170** (1HKOs Tapu Lele-GX).
