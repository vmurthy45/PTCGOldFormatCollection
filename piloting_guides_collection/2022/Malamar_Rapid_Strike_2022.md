# How to Pilot — Malamar Rapid Strike (2022)

_Format: Sword & Shield-on Standard 2022 (CRE–on). A Psychic **Malamar** engine feeding Rapid
Strike / Psychic attackers, backed by the Inteleon (Shady Dealings) toolbox._

## One-line identity
A Psychic-acceleration toolbox: **Malamar's Psychic Recharge** streams **Psychic Energy from
the discard** onto your bench every turn, powering a rotating cast of attackers, while the
**Inteleon engine** (Shady Dealings + Quick Shooting) searches your Trainers and pings extra
damage. **Spiral Energy** fuels the Rapid Strike side; you set up fast and hit from multiple
angles.

## The meta it lived in
2022 Standard was a VSTAR/VMAX world (Arceus, Mew, Palkia, Urshifu). This Malamar build is the
non-VMAX, engine-driven option: Psychic Recharge gives it acceleration and resilience that the
big-Basic decks lack, and the Inteleon package (searching + Quick Shooting spread) supplies the
consistency and chip damage. It's a flexible, techable deck that adapts its attacker to the
matchup rather than relying on one big hitter.

## Engine cards (why they're here)
- **Inkay (69/198) ×4 → Malamar (70/198) ×4** — the engine. **Psychic Recharge** (Ability): once
  per turn per Malamar, attach a Psychic from the **discard** to a benched Psychic Pokémon —
  multiple Malamar = a flood of Energy every turn. The heart of the deck; get 2–3 online.
- **Sobble (41/198) ×4 → Drizzile (56/202) ×3 → Inteleon (58/202) ×1 + Inteleon (43/198) ×1** —
  the dual engine: **Shady Dealings** searches Trainers on evolve, and **Inteleon (43/198)
  Quick Shooting** pings **20 to any Pokémon** each turn — the chip damage that lines up KOs.
- **Remoraid (36/163) ×2 → Octillery (37/163) ×2** — **Rapid Strike Search** (Ability): search
  any Rapid Strike card (a Rapid Strike attacker, Spiral Energy, Tower of Waters) — consistency
  for the Rapid Strike side.
- **Fog Crystal ×4** — search a Psychic Pokémon **or** a Psychic Energy — the tutor that finds
  Inkay/attackers *and* fuels Psychic Recharge; the deck's key consistency Item.

## Support / tech (why the odd counts)
- **Cynthia's Ambition ×4 / Korrina's Focus ×4 / Brawly ×4** — a heavy, redundant draw suite:
  **Cynthia's Ambition** (draw to 6, or 8 after a KO), **Korrina's Focus** (draw to hand-size
  +? Rapid Strike Supporter), **Brawly** (search 2 Fighting/Rapid Strike Pokémon). Lots of draw
  keeps the engine flowing without a Bibarel/Crobat Ability (which Path to the Peak would shut
  off).
- **Evolution Incense ×4 / Level Ball ×4** — search the Inkay/Malamar and Inteleon/Octillery
  lines (Level Ball grabs the low-HP Basics/Stage-1s efficiently).
- **Scoop Up Net ×4** — re-trigger Shady Dealings / pick up a chipped Pokémon / reset a piece —
  the toolbox loop.
- **Rescue Carrier ×2 / Ordinary Rod ×1** — recover Pokémon/Energy (recur Malamar and Psychic
  Energy for the long game).
- **Escape Rope ×1 / Tower of Waters ×1** — pivot / free retreat for Rapid Strike Pokémon.
- **4 Spiral Energy + 2 Psychic Energy** — **Spiral Energy** provides Psychic *and* Fighting-type
  Energy (fuels both the Psychic Recharge targets and Rapid Strike/Fighting attackers); basic
  Psychic feeds Recharge directly.

## Ideal early game
- **Turn 1:** bench **Inkay(s)** + Sobble + Remoraid; **Fog Crystal** to find a piece/Energy; get
  Psychic into the discard so Recharge is live.
- **Turn 2:** evolve **Malamar** (start Psychic Recharge) and an **Inteleon** (Shady Dealings),
  and begin streaming Energy onto your attacker while Quick Shooting chips.

## The core loop
Get 2–3 **Malamar** online → **Psychic Recharge** floods Energy from the discard every turn →
power your chosen attacker and swing, while **Inteleon Quick Shooting** pings 20 to set up KOs
→ **Fog Crystal / Octillery** keep you stocked, **Scoop Up Net** re-triggers your Abilities, and
the heavy draw suite refuels. You adapt the attacker to the matchup and grind from multiple
angles.

## Priorities / rules of thumb
- **2–3 Malamar is the setup goal** — one isn't enough acceleration; protect them (they're the
  engine).
- **Keep Psychic in the discard** — Recharge pulls from there; use Fog Crystal/draw to seed it.
- **Quick Shooting pings set up KOs** — place the 20 where it converts a 2HKO into a KO or kills
  an engine Basic.
- **Draw over Abilities** — the deck leans on Supporter draw (Cynthia/Korrina/Brawly) so it's
  resilient to Path to the Peak; use them freely.

## Weaknesses / bad matchups
- **Darkness (Malamar/Psychic attackers are Dark-Weak in spots)** — Dark decks punish the Psychic
  side.
- **Fast OHKO VMAX decks (Mew)** — can out-tempo the engine setup before it stabilizes.
- **Bench snipe / disruption** — your low-HP engine (Inkay, Sobble, Remoraid) is vulnerable to
  Urshifu/Inteleon spread before it evolves.
- **Consistency variance** — a multi-line engine deck; a clunky start (no Fog Crystal/Malamar)
  falls behind.

## Quick reference — the numbers
- Psychic Recharge: **1 Psychic from discard → benched Psychic Pokémon**, per Malamar, per turn.
- Quick Shooting (Inteleon): **20 to any Pokémon** per turn; Shady Dealings searches Trainers on
  evolve.
- Fog Crystal: search **a Psychic Pokémon or a Psychic Energy**.
- Spiral Energy: provides **Psychic and Fighting** Energy; setup goal **2–3 Malamar**.
