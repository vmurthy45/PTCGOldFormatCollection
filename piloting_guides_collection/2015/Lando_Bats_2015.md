# How to Pilot — Lando Bats (2015)

_Format: BCR-on 2015 (XY "Fighting aggro" era). "Landobats" — this build mirrors Rowan
Stavenow's Junior World Champion list._

## One-line identity
Fast Fighting aggression: cheap, hard-hitting attackers (**Landorus-EX**,
**Hawlucha**, **Lucario-EX**) boosted by **Strong Energy / Fighting Stadium / Muscle
Band**, plus a **Zubat → Golbat → Crobat** line that drops free damage counters every
time it evolves. You spread and snipe, then clean up KOs a turn early.

## The meta it lived in
2015 rewarded fast, cheap damage, and Landorus was the dominant Fighting attacker —
preying on Darkrai/Yveltal Weakness and, with the Bats, applying pressure every single
turn. It won the Junior World Championship and put multiple copies into Worlds top cut.
The Bats' evolve-damage is what makes the deck "hit every turn without attacking."

## Engine cards (why they're here)
- **Landorus-EX (89/149) ×2** — the star. **Hammerhead**: 30 to the Active + 30 to a
  benched Pokémon for a single Energy — cheap, repeatable spread that sets up KOs.
  **Land's Judgment** (3 Energy) for a big finish. With Strong Energy + Fighting Stadium
  + Muscle Band it hits hard fast.
- **Hawlucha (63/111) ×4** — non-EX attacker; for one Energy it does **80 to EXs (+
  more with Strong Energy/Stadium)** — 100 vs. an EX with support, giving up only 1
  prize. Free retreat. Your efficient EX-killer.
- **Zubat (31/119) ×4 → Golbat (32/119) ×3 → Crobat (33/119) ×3** — the damage engine.
  **Sneaky Bite** (Golbat) puts 2 counters and **Surprise Bite** (Crobat) puts 3
  counters on any opposing Pokémon *when you evolve it* — free damage each turn to soften
  targets. The whole line has no retreat cost.
- **Lucario-EX (54/111) ×1** — a second Fighting attacker with a different Weakness and
  three attacks; **Corkscrew Smash** damages + draws. Good vs. Seismitoad matchups.
- **Shaymin-EX (77/108) ×1** — **Set Up**: draw to 6; the consistency engine.

## Support / tech (why the odd counts)
- **Super Scoop Up ×4** — pick up a Golbat/Crobat to **re-use its Bite** on replay
  (more free damage), or save a damaged attacker. Flip-based, hence 4.
- **Scoop Up Cyclone (95/101, ACE SPEC) ×1** — a *guaranteed* scoop (no flip) to re-
  trigger a Bat's damage or dodge a KO.
- **Muscle Band ×4** — +20; core to the KO math.
- **Fighting Stadium ×3** — +20 when your Fighting Pokémon attack an EX (big with
  Hawlucha/Landorus).
- **Strong Energy ×4** — +20 damage when attached to a Fighting Pokémon; **5 Fighting**
  round out the Energy.
- **Silent Lab ×2** — Stadium that shuts off Abilities on Basic Pokémon (hurts
  Shaymin-EX/Jirachi-EX-reliant decks) — a counter-Stadium and disruption piece.
- **Korrina ×2** — search a Fighting Pokémon **and** a Fighting Item (Muscle Band, etc.)
  in one card — huge consistency.
- **Juniper ×4 / N ×2 / Colress ×2 / AZ ×1** — draw; AZ picks up a Bat to re-Bite or
  saves a Pokémon.
- **Ultra Ball ×4 / VS Seeker ×2 / Lysandre ×2** — search, Supporter recursion, and gust
  targets for KOs.

## Ideal early game
- **Turn 1:** Shaymin **Set Up**, bench Zubat(s) and a Landorus/Hawlucha; use Korrina to
  grab an attacker + Muscle Band. **Hammerhead** to start chipping (30 active + 30 bench)
  and evolve a Zubat if you can for a free Bite.
- **Early turns:** stack evolve-damage (Golbat/Crobat) + Hammerhead snipe so multiple
  opposing Pokémon are in KO range.

## The core loop
Each turn: **Hammerhead** for spread, **evolve/replay Bats** (via Super Scoop Up / Scoop
Up Cyclone / AZ) to keep dropping free counters, and use **Hawlucha** (+ Strong Energy /
Fighting Stadium / Muscle Band) to 1HKO EXs cheaply. **Lysandre** drags the softened
target up. You take prizes faster than the opponent can stabilize.

## Priorities / rules of thumb
- **Spread first, then convert** — Hammerhead + Bites set up multi-prize turns; don't
  rush a single KO when you can set up two.
- **Re-use the Bats** — Super Scoop Up / Scoop Up Cyclone / AZ replaying a Crobat is
  extra free damage; that's the deck's engine.
- **Hawlucha kills EXs for 1 prize** — lean on it so the prize trade favors you.
- **Korrina is your setup button** — it finds attacker + Item together.

## Weaknesses / bad matchups
- **Seismitoad-EX (Item lock)** — Quaking Punch shuts off Super Scoop Up and your Item
  engine; lean on Lucario-EX and Supporters. A real grind.
- **Grass attackers** — Landorus-EX is Grass-Weak (Leafeon/other Grass punish it).
- **Bench-damage protection (Mr. Mime)** — blunts Hammerhead's snipe and the Bites.
- **Silent Lab cuts both ways** — it can shut off *your* Shaymin-EX too; sequence its
  use.

## Quick reference — the numbers
- Hammerhead: **30 active + 30 bench snipe** for 1 Energy.
- Hawlucha: **80 to EXs** base (+ Strong Energy / Fighting Stadium / Muscle Band → ~100+),
  single-prize.
- Golbat/Crobat on evolve: **2 / 3 damage counters** on any opposing Pokémon.
- Boosters: Strong Energy **+20**, Fighting Stadium **+20 vs. EX**, Muscle Band **+20**.
