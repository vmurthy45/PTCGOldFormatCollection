# How to Pilot — Archie's Blastoise (2015)

_Format: BCR-on 2015 (XY "Item-heavy combo" era). "ArchieStoise" / "HonorStoise" — this
is Jacob Van Wagner's 2015 World Championship-**winning** list, essentially card-for-
card._

## One-line identity
A turn-1 combo deck: dump your hand with Item draw, discard a **Blastoise** with
**Battle Compressor / Ultra Ball**, then **Archie's Ace in the Hole** puts it straight
into play on turn 1. Now **Deluge** pours Water Energy onto **Keldeo-EX** for a giant
**Secret Sword** — often attacking for 110+ on the first turn.

## The meta it lived in
2015 was full of Item lock (Seismitoad) and disruption — yet ArchieStoise thrived
*because* it did everything in one explosive turn before the lock mattered. Once
Blastoise was down and Keldeo/Wailord were healing off Lasers with Rough Seas,
Seismitoad's 30-damage Quaking Punch couldn't keep up. It won Worlds 2015 (Masters).

## Engine cards (why they're here)
- **Archie's Ace in the Hole (124/160) ×2** — the combo enabler. If your hand is down to
  exactly this card (0 others after playing it), put a **Blastoise** from the discard
  onto your bench. Reuse it with **VS Seeker**.
- **Blastoise (31/149) ×2** — **Deluge**: attach any number of Water Energy from hand to
  your Pokémon each turn. It's the whole engine — and you want a 2nd line, since losing
  your only Blastoise kills the deck. You *discard* it, then Archie it into play.
- **Keldeo-EX (49/149) ×3** — the main attacker. **Secret Sword** scales with Water
  attached (Deluge dumps a pile in one turn → 110+ turn 1, or a 1HKO later). **Rush In**
  free pivot.
- **Jirachi-EX (60/101) ×2** — **Stellar Guidance**: search a Supporter — usually to
  find **Archie's** for the combo. A key consistency piece.
- **Shaymin-EX (77/108) ×2** — **Set Up**: draw until you have 6 — the engine that lets
  you burn your whole hand down to just Archie's.

## The tech attackers (why the odd 1-ofs)
- **Articuno (17/108) ×1** — steals an extra prize vs. single-prize decks (Night March)
  via its era text; a nifty prize-tempo tech.
- **Wailord-EX (38/160) ×1** — a 250-HP wall that heals with Rough Seas; miserable for
  Seismitoad to deal with.
- **Mewtwo-EX (54/113) ×1** — off-type attacker (X-Ball) that still benefits from Deluge;
  answers opposing Mewtwo/Garbodor.
- **Exeggcute (4/116) ×2** — **Propagation**: replay it from the discard as a bench body/
  Ultra Ball fodder without losing a card — grease for the combo turn.

## Support / tech (why the counts)
- **Battle Compressor ×4** — discard Blastoise + tech Pokémon + Supporters straight into
  the discard to set up Archie's and VS Seeker. The single most important consistency
  Item.
- **VS Seeker ×4** — replay Archie's/Juniper/Lysandre from the discard; the reason 2
  Archie's is enough.
- **Ultra Ball ×4 / Acro Bike ×4 / Trainers' Mail ×4** — dig fast and discard freely to
  empty your hand to Archie's-only.
- **Superior Energy Retrieval ×4** — return 4 Water from discard so Deluge never runs
  dry.
- **Computer Search (ACE SPEC) ×1 / Float Stone ×1 / Muscle Band ×1** — grab any card,
  free retreat, +20.
- **Rough Seas ×2** — heal 30 off Keldeo/Wailord between turns (huge vs. Laser/Toad).
- **Juniper ×2 / Lysandre ×1 / N ×1** — minimal Supporter line (you find them via Jirachi
  + VS Seeker); Lysandre drags up KO targets.
- **11 Water** — the fuel for Deluge + Secret Sword.

## Ideal early game (the combo turn)
- **Turn 1:** Shaymin **Set Up** to draw into the engine → **Battle Compressor** a
  Blastoise (and junk) into the discard → **Ultra Ball / Acro Bike / Trainers' Mail** to
  burn your hand down to exactly **Archie's Ace in the Hole** → play it, Blastoise hits
  the bench → **Deluge** a pile of Water onto **Keldeo-EX** → **Secret Sword** for 110+.
- If the T1 combo isn't there, you can fall back on a slower Blastoise line, but the
  deck wants the explosive open.

## The core loop
Once Blastoise is down: each turn **Deluge** Water onto Keldeo (refilled by **Superior
Energy Retrieval**) → **Secret Sword** for a KO → **Rough Seas** heals your Active →
**Lysandre** drags up the next target. Swap to Wailord/Articuno/Mewtwo when the matchup
calls for it.

## Priorities / rules of thumb
- **Hand management is everything** — you must reduce to exactly Archie's (nothing else)
  to trigger it; sequence Compressor/Ultra Ball/Bike carefully.
- **Protect a 2nd Blastoise** — one Blastoise KO'd without a backup ends your engine.
- **Jirachi finds Archie's** — treat it as your combo tutor.
- **Watch for the whiff** — like any combo deck, a Jirachi/Archie's prized or a bad
  Shaymin draw can flatline you; know your outs before committing.

## Weaknesses / bad matchups
- **Itself** — it's a combo deck; bad opening hands or a prized Jirachi/Archie's cost
  games.
- **Leafeon-type "energy on board" punishers** — decks that exploit your huge Energy
  count.
- **Heavy early disruption before the combo lands** (a T1 Seismitoad lock the turn you
  needed Items) can stumble you — though once Blastoise is down, Toad is a good matchup.

## Quick reference — the numbers
- Archie's: put a Blastoise into play **if your hand is only Archie's after playing it**.
- Deluge: attach **any number of Water from hand** per turn.
- Secret Sword: scales with Water on Keldeo (**~7 = 1HKO a Basic EX**; 110+ turn 1).
- Combo target: **turn-1 Blastoise → Secret Sword**.
