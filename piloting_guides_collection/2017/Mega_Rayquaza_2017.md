# How to Pilot — Mega Rayquaza (2017)

_Format: Sun & Moon-on Standard, **late 2017 / Worlds era** (Burning Shadows — note Guzma/
Tapu Lele-GX). A last-hurrah build of the M Rayquaza-EX turbo deck before it rotated._

## One-line identity
Fast, explosive tempo: **Sky Field** expands your bench to 8, and **M Rayquaza-EX's
Emerald Break** does 30 × your benched Pokémon — up to 240 for a single DCE. Flood the
bench with **Shaymin-EX / Hoopa-EX** draw engines, Mega-evolve for free with **Rayquaza
Spirit Link**, and 1HKO the format from turn 2.

## The meta it lived in
This is Mega Rayquaza's final Standard season (it rotated after 2017). It was still a
raw-power tempo deck that punished slow starts, but the metagame had adapted: **Parallel
City** (shrinks your bench to 3, gutting Emerald Break) and **Garbodor Garbotoxin** (shuts
off Shaymin/Hoopa/Dragonite) were everywhere, which is why this list packs **Field Blower**
and **Hex Maniac** to fight through the hate.

## Engine cards (why they're here)
- **Rayquaza-EX (75/108) ×4 → M Rayquaza-EX (76/108) ×3** — the core. **Emerald Break**:
  30 × your benched Pokémon (8 bench under Sky Field = 240) for a single DCE. It's a Mega
  (evolving normally ends your turn — Spirit Link fixes that).
- **Rayquaza Spirit Link ×2** — Mega-evolve **without ending your turn** — essential to
  evolve *and* attack the same turn.
- **Sky Field ×4** — Stadium: **8-Pokémon bench** (vs. the normal 5). More bench = more
  Emerald Break damage *and* more room for draw engines. You want it down and to win the
  Stadium war (hence Field Blower).
- **Shaymin-EX (77/108) ×4** — **Set Up**: draw to 6. Both a draw engine *and* a benched
  body that adds 30 to Emerald Break. The heart of the turbo plan.
- **Hoopa-EX (36/98) ×2** — **Scoundrel Ring**: pull 3 EXs (Rayquaza/Shaymin) from the deck
  — instant bench-flood + setup.

## Support / tech (why the odd counts)
- **Tapu Lele-GX (60/145) ×2** — Wonder Tag for any Supporter (N, Guzma, Skyla); also a
  bench body.
- **Tapu Koko-GX (47/145) ×1 + Tapu Koko (SM30) ×1** — Lightning attackers/**Flying Flip**
  snipe; Tapu Koko-GX's **Aerial Ace**/**GX** gives a second angle and free-retreat body.
- **Sudowoodo (66/145) ×1** — bench-snipe / anti-Stage-2 tech.
- **Sycamore ×3 / Guzma ×2 / Hex Maniac ×2 / N ×1 / Skyla ×1 / Pokémon Fan Club ×1** — draw
  + disruption. **Hex Maniac** turns off the opponent's Abilities (protect your combo turn/
  answer their engine); **Guzma** gusts the KO; **Fan Club** bench-floods Basics.
- **VS Seeker ×3 / Ultra Ball ×4 / Mega Turbo ×3 / Field Blower ×2 / Rescue Stretcher ×1 /
  Float Stone ×2** — recursion, search, **Mega Turbo** (accelerate Energy onto Rayquaza
  from the discard), remove **Parallel City/Garbodor's Tool**, recover, free retreat.
- **Choice Band ×2** — +30 vs. GX/EX to top off KO numbers.
- **5 Lightning + 4 DCE** — DCE powers Emerald Break in one attach; Lightning for Tapu
  Koko/off-costs.

## Ideal early game
- **Turn 1:** **Sky Field** down, **Shaymin Set Up** and **Hoopa Scoundrel Ring** to flood
  the bench, get a Rayquaza-EX active.
- **Turn 2:** **Spirit Link → M Rayquaza-EX** (turn doesn't end), attach **DCE** (or Mega
  Turbo), and **Emerald Break** for 180–240 — an immediate 1HKO.

## The core loop
Keep the bench full (Shaymin/Hoopa/Tapu) under **Sky Field**, Mega-evolve free with Spirit
Link, and **Emerald Break** for a 1HKO every turn. **Mega Turbo** re-arms a fresh Rayquaza
after one is KO'd; **Guzma** picks the target; **Hex Maniac / Field Blower** clear the
Ability-lock and Parallel City that try to shrink your damage. Race them before they set
up.

## Priorities / rules of thumb
- **Protect Sky Field** — Field Blower/extra Sky Field to win the Stadium war; Parallel City
  down = your damage collapses.
- **Full bench = lethal** — 6–8 benched Pokémon is the difference between 180 and 240; don't
  under-develop.
- **Hex Maniac through Garbotoxin** — if Garbodor is up, Hex/Field Blower restores your
  Shaymin/Hoopa draw for the key turn.
- **Mind the Shaymin liability** — a full bench of EXs means Guzma/Emerald-Break-back can
  punish you; sequence so you're not handing easy prizes.

## Weaknesses / bad matchups
- **Parallel City** — the defining counter: bench to 3 = Emerald Break does ~90. You *must*
  answer it.
- **Garbodor (Garbotoxin)** — turns off Shaymin/Hoopa/Dragonite; your setup engine dies
  without Hex/Blower.
- **Lightning / weakness hits** — M Rayquaza is Lightning-Weak (Tapu Koko-GX, Vikavolt 1HKO
  it).
- **Fast disruption / N to small hands** — a turbo deck that bricks if its draw engines are
  denied.

## Quick reference — the numbers
- Emerald Break: **30 × your benched Pokémon** (8 = 240) for 1 DCE.
- Sky Field: **8-Pokémon bench**.
- Spirit Link: **Mega-evolve without ending your turn**.
- Choice Band **+30 vs. GX/EX**; Mega Turbo: Energy from discard → Rayquaza.
