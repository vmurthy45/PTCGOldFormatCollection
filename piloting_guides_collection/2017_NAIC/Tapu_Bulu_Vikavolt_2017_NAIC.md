# How to Pilot — Tapu Bulu/Vikavolt (2017 NAIC)

_Format: **PRC–GRI Standard** (June 2017 NAIC meta — pre-Burning Shadows, **Lysandre**
not Guzma). "BuluVolt" — a Day-2 NAIC deck. A Grass/Lightning energy-acceleration
toolbox._

## One-line identity
Turbo one-shots: **Vikavolt's Strong Charge** attaches a Grass *and* a Lightning Energy
from your deck every turn (an Ability), letting **Tapu Bulu-GX** fire **Nature's Judgment**
for 180 (210 with Choice Band) from turn 2 — enough to 1HKO almost anything. Set up one
Vikavolt and you stream giant hitters all game.

## The meta it lived in
NAIC 2017 (PRC-GRI): Vespiquen, Metagross-GX, Garbodor variants, Greninja, Volcanion,
Zoroark/Drampa. BuluVolt's appeal was raw, weakness-independent power — **Tapu Bulu-GX has
no Weakness**, and Nature's Judgment 1HKOs the format's 180-HP GXs. It hit Day 2 at NAIC
and put up multiple Regional results. The catch: it's a multi-Stage-2 setup deck whose
engine (Strong Charge) folds to Ability lock, so consistency and playing around Garbodor
are everything.

## Engine cards (why they're here)
- **Grubbin (13/149) ×3 → Charjabug (51/149) ×1 → Vikavolt (52/149) ×3** — the engine.
  **Strong Charge** (Ability): once per turn per Vikavolt, attach 1 Grass + 1 Lightning from
  your *deck* to your Pokémon. Two Vikavolt = 4 Energy a turn. The whole deck exists to get
  Vikavolt online (usually via Rare Candy). Note: Strong Charge pulls from the **deck**, so
  discarded Energy is dead — hence Energy Recycler.
- **Tapu Bulu-GX (SM32) ×3** — the main attacker. **Nature's Judgment**: 180, discard all
  Energy on it (Strong Charge refuels next turn). **Tapu Wilderness-GX**: heal all damage +
  a KO in some lines. No Weakness = it trades up against everything.
- **Rare Candy ×3** — jump Grubbin → Vikavolt a turn faster; turn-2 Vikavolt is the goal.
- **Energy Recycler ×2** — shuffle 5 Energy from discard back into the deck — critical,
  since Nature's Judgment discards Energy and Strong Charge only grabs from the *deck*.

## Support / tech (why the odd counts)
- **Tapu Koko (SM30) ×1** — **Flying Flip** snipe (soften Ralts/benched targets so Nature's
  Judgment KOs without discarding) + free-retreat pivot; also a Lightning body.
- **Tapu Lele-GX (60/145) ×3** — Wonder Tag: any Supporter (Brigette, Skyla, Lysandre, N).
- **Sycamore ×4 / N ×3 / Lysandre ×2 / Skyla ×1 / Professor Kukui ×1 / Brigette ×1** — draw
  + search + reach. **Skyla** guarantees the Rare Candy / Heavy Ball for turn-2 Vikavolt;
  **Lysandre** (your only gust pre-Guzma) drags the KO; **Kukui** adds +20 for exact numbers.
- **VS Seeker ×4 / Ultra Ball ×4 / Heavy Ball ×1 / Float Stone ×2 / Field Blower ×2** —
  search, recursion, get Vikavolt pieces, free retreat, and **Field Blower** to clear
  Garbotoxin (which kills Strong Charge) and Parallel City.
- **Choice Band ×2 / Fighting Fury Belt ×2** — Band pushes Nature's Judgment to 210 (1HKOs
  most GX); **Fighting Fury Belt** adds HP so Tapu Bulu survives a hit *and* lets it KO
  without discarding Energy in some spots.
- **7 Grass + 5 Lightning** — both types are mandatory for Strong Charge; the higher Grass
  count feeds Nature's Judgment.

## Ideal early game
- **Turn 1:** **Brigette** (via Tapu Lele) for Grubbin(s) + Tapu Koko; attach an Energy,
  pass. A Tapu Koko **Flying Flip** here helps future KO math.
- **Turn 2 (the key turn):** **Skyla/draw → Rare Candy → Vikavolt**, **Strong Charge** onto
  Tapu Bulu-GX, and **Nature's Judgment for 180** (210 with Choice Band).

## The core loop
Get a Vikavolt (ideally two) online → **Strong Charge** to load a Tapu Bulu-GX every turn →
**Nature's Judgment for 180–210** to 1HKO whatever's Active → **Energy Recycler** the
discarded Energy back so you never run dry → **Lysandre** to pick the target. With two
Vikavolt you can even attack while building the next Bulu. No Weakness means you out-trade
the field.

## Priorities / rules of thumb
- **Turn-2 Vikavolt is everything** — Skyla + Rare Candy is the line; a whiff here loses
  tempo you can't recover. Set up **two** Vikavolt when possible so a Lysandre/KO on one
  doesn't stop you.
- **Energy Recycler before you run dry** — Strong Charge only grabs from the deck; recycle
  discarded Energy proactively.
- **Field Blower vs. Garbodor** — Garbotoxin shuts off Strong Charge (your engine); clearing
  it is often the game.
- **Soften with Tapu Koko / Kukui** so Nature's Judgment can KO *without* discarding Energy
  when you're low.

## Weaknesses / bad matchups
- **Garbodor (Garbotoxin) / Hex Maniac** — turns off Strong Charge; the engine dies. Field
  Blower and careful sequencing are mandatory (Tapu Bulu's Grass hits Garbodor... but
  Garbotoxin still stops your acceleration).
- **Greninja BREAK** — **Shadow Stitching** locks your Abilities *and* they snipe your
  Grubbin/Vikavolt; a rough matchup.
- **Decidueye/Vileplume** — Vileplume Item-lock stops Rare Candy; hard to set up Vikavolt.
- **Slow starts** — a two-Stage-2 line (Vikavolt + evolving attackers) can brick; a prized
  Brigette/Rare Candy hurts.

## Quick reference — the numbers
- Strong Charge: **1 Grass + 1 Lightning from deck → your Pokémon**, per Vikavolt, per turn.
- Nature's Judgment: **180** (discard all Energy on Bulu) → 210 with Choice Band.
- Setup: **turn-2 Vikavolt** via Skyla + Rare Candy.
- Tapu Bulu-GX: **no Weakness**; Gust: **Lysandre only** (pre-Guzma).
