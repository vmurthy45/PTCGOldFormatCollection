# How to Pilot — Gyarados (2010)

_Format: HGSS-on 2010. A non-SP "rogue" that punched into the SP-dominated meta by
attacking for **zero Energy**._

## One-line identity
Discard your own Magikarp, then swing with **Gyarados** for free: Tail Revenge does
50 damage per Magikarp in the discard, so a full discard = a huge, energy-less hit on
a Stage 1. Cheap, fast, and full of tech room because you're not paying for Energy.

## The meta it lived in
The format leaned hard on low-HP Basics and Pokémon SP (LuxChomp everywhere). A deck
that hits 110+ for zero Energy on turn 2, on a bulky Stage 1, was a genuine problem
for that field — and Expert Belt pushed it to 150 HP / higher damage. Gyarados had
already top-cut 2009 Worlds and stayed strong into 2010–2011.

## Engine cards (why they're here)
- **Gyarados (19/100)** — main attacker. **Tail Revenge**: 50 × number of Magikarp in
  your discard pile, for no Energy. Three Magikarp down = 150 (or the classic ~110 with
  Belt math per era discussion).
- **Magikarp (65/100) ×4** — you *want* these in the discard, not in play. They're
  ammo.
- **Sableye (48/100) ×4** — the setup enabler. **Impersonate** copies a Supporter from
  hand as your first-turn play — usually **Pokémon Collector** turn 1.
- **Felicity's Drawing (98/106) ×4** — draw that **discards** to draw more — the primary
  way to dump Magikarp into the discard on turn 2.
- **Broken Time-Space (104/127) ×4** — Stadium that lets you evolve the turn a Pokémon
  is played/comes down — Magikarp → Gyarados with no wait, enabling turn-2 (or faster)
  swings.
- **Combee (79/130)** — bench-filler / setup (Honey Gather-style draw) that helps you
  churn to your pieces.

## The tech suite (why the odd 1-ofs)
- **Luxray GL + Luxray GL LV.X (1-1)** — after a Gyarados trades, promote Luxray, use
  **Bright Look** to drag up a specific threat and KO it. Adds reach the pure Gyarados
  shell lacks.
- **Uxie / Azelf / Mesprit (LA pixies)** — Uxie draw; **Azelf** digs a prized Magikarp
  back (critical — a prized Magikarp is lost damage); Mesprit for a turn of Power denial.
- **Crobat G ×2 + Poké Turn ×4** — free Flash Bite damage counters to reach KO numbers,
  recycled with Poké Turn.
- **Pokémon Rescue ×4 / Super Scoop Up ×4** — recover Gyarados/Magikarp; SSU also picks
  up a damaged Gyarados to reset it.
- **Cyclone Energy / Warp Energy** — attached "switch" effects: Cyclone forces the
  opponent to switch (drag up a target), Warp lets you retreat free.
- **Expert Belt ×2** — +20 damage / +20 HP; turns Gyarados into a 150-HP threat.

## Ideal early game
- **Turn 1:** bench Magikarp + Sableye; **Impersonate → Pokémon Collector** to load
  Basics (grab Magikarps, a pixie, Combee).
- **Turn 2:** **Felicity's Drawing** to discard 2 Magikarp (with Broken Time-Space out),
  evolve a Magikarp into Gyarados, and **Tail Revenge** for a big energy-free hit.

## The core loop
Keep Magikarp count in the discard high (3 = 150 damage), attack for free every turn,
and use Luxray GL LV.X / Cyclone Energy / Crobat G to make sure the damage lands where
it wins prizes. Rescue/SSU keep the Gyarados coming when they trade.

## Priorities / rules of thumb
- **Discard is your Energy.** Protect the count — don't let all your Magikarp get stuck
  in hand or prizes (Azelf fixes prizes).
- **Free retreat + Cyclone/Warp** = you dictate the Active matchup. Use it to dodge or
  to pull up a target.
- Save **Luxray Bright Look** for the moment it steals a clean prize, not turn 2 filler.

## Weaknesses / bad matchups
- **Lightning weakness** — Luxray GL LV.X (in opposing SP decks) hits Gyarados for
  weakness; be careful trading into their Luxray.
- **Prized / hand-clogged Magikarp** — a slow discard start means weak Tail Revenge;
  mulligan-y hands hurt.
- **Judge / hand disruption** at the wrong moment can stall the Felicity's engine.

## Quick reference — the numbers
- Tail Revenge: **50 × Magikarp in discard**, **0 Energy**. 3 Magikarp = 150.
- Expert Belt: **+20 damage / +20 HP** (Gyarados → 150 HP).
- Turn-2 attacker is the plan (Broken Time-Space + Felicity's Drawing).
- Energy is all utility (Cyclone/Warp) — no Energy needed to attack.
