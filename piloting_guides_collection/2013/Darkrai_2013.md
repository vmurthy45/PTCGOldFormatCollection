# How to Pilot — Darkrai (2013)

_Format: BW-on 2013 (Plasma Storm era). A "Speed Darkrai" / Dark Cloak build with the
Hypnotoxic Laser + Virbank City Gym damage engine. This build mirrors Jason Klaczynski's
2013 World Championship-**winning** list — his record third Worlds title._

## One-line identity
The consistency king of the format: **Darkrai-EX** with **Dark Cloak** free retreat
and **Night Spear** snipe, supercharged by **Hypnotoxic Laser + Virbank City Gym** so
your attacks hit KO numbers a full turn early. Fast, flexible, and hard to disrupt —
built almost entirely from staples.

## The meta it lived in
2013's format rewarded decks that could 1HKO EXs (RayEels, Black Kyurem/Blastoise) or
grind efficiently (Darkrai). Darkrai's edge was **LaserBank**: a Night Spear + Dark
Claw + Laser + Virbank does 140, enough to 1HKO a Blastoise; add the 30 snipe and it
1HKOs Keldeo-EX, Mewtwo-EX, or Tornadus-EX. It's the deck other decks had to beat — widely
considered the **best deck in the format (BDIF)**, and Jason Klaczynski piloted it to the 2013
World Championship (his third Worlds title, a record at the time).

## Engine cards (why they're here)
- **Darkrai-EX (63/108) ×4** — the core. **Dark Cloak**: all your Darkness-carrying
  Pokémon retreat free. **Night Spear**: 90 Active + 30 bench snipe. You run four so a
  prized copy never stops the plan and you always have a backup vs. surprise Dragon
  Bursts.
- **Sableye (62/108) ×3** — the setup MVP. **Junk Hunt**: return two Item cards from
  discard to hand. Free-retreat under Dark Cloak, it recurs Lasers, Catchers, Energy
  Switch — a near-infinite Item engine that also gives donk potential (Confuse Ray +
  Dark Claw + Laser can KO a small Basic).
- **Keldeo-EX (49/149) ×2** — **Rush In** free pivot to escape Status/Catcher; a Water
  attacker when needed. Note: it's the same card that answers *opposing* status locks.
- **Hypnotoxic Laser ×4 + Virbank City Gym ×2** — the damage engine: poison (20 between
  turns with Virbank) + a sleep flip. Pushes Night Spear to lethal numbers.
- **Dark Claw ×2** — +20 to Darkrai's attacks; part of the 140-damage LaserBank math.

## Support / tech (why the odd counts)
- **Dark Patch ×4** — accelerate Darkness from the discard onto benched Darkrai; the
  reason you can 1HKO from a standing start.
- **Energy Switch ×3** — move Darkness around (e.g. borrow from a free-retreating
  Sableye) to hit the Turn-2 Night Spear.
- **Professor Juniper ×4 / N ×4 / Random Receiver ×3 / Bicycle ×2** — the draw spine;
  Juniper discards Darkness to feed Dark Patch, N disrupts.
- **Ultra Ball ×3 / Computer Search (ACE SPEC) ×1** — search + more Darkness discard.
- **Pokémon Catcher ×4** — drag targets for Night Spear / to remove support Basics.
- **Enhanced Hammer ×2** — strip Special Energy (great vs. Plasma's Blend/Plasma/Prism
  and vs. DCE decks).
- **Mr. Mime (47/116) ×1** — **Bench Barrier**: blocks bench snipe damage, protecting
  your own board from mirror Night Spears.
- **Tool Scrapper ×1** — remove opposing Tools (or Garbotoxin's).
- **10 Darkness** — the fuel for Dark Patch + Night Spear.

## Ideal early game
- **Turn 1:** ideally start Sableye; **Junk Hunt** to bank Items and set the engine.
  Bench a Darkrai, discard Darkness (Juniper/Ultra Ball) to arm Dark Patch.
- **Turn 2:** Dark Patch + attach (often via Energy Switch off Sableye) to reach a
  **Night Spear**, placing the 30 snipe where it sets up a future KO.

## The core loop
End every turn with a Night Spear (90 + 30 snipe). Layer **Laser + Virbank + Dark
Claw** to hit exact 1HKO numbers on the format's EXs. Use Dark Cloak free retreat to
rotate attackers, **Sableye Junk Hunt** to recur Lasers/Catchers indefinitely, and
Enhanced Hammer to strip the special Energy that fast decks depend on.

## Priorities / rules of thumb
- **Keep two Darkrai ready** so a surprise 1HKO (Dragon Burst, etc.) doesn't end your
  offense.
- **Sableye is your engine** — protect it and abuse Junk Hunt; late game it retrieves
  the exact Item you need.
- **Do the LaserBank math** before attacking: know whether Claw+Laser+Virbank gets you
  to the KO this turn or next.
- Consider **Switch** for the mirror and Garbodor (retains Energy, clears Status).

## Weaknesses / bad matchups
- **Fighting decks (Landorus-EX)** — Darkrai is Fighting-Weak; Landorus pressures you
  from turn 1. Lasers help you race, but it's tough.
- **Blastoise/Keldeo-EX** — Squirtle BCR blunts the snipe and Keldeo out-tanks; you
  need LaserBank to keep pace.
- **Garbodor (Garbotoxin)** — shuts off Dark Cloak (free retreat) and Dark Patch; lean
  on Tool Scrapper and Switch.

## Quick reference — the numbers
- Night Spear: **90 active + 30 bench snipe**.
- LaserBank max: Night Spear + Dark Claw + Laser + Virbank = **140** (+30 snipe →
  1HKOs Keldeo/Mewtwo/Tornadus).
- Poison w/ Virbank: **20 between turns**.
- Setup: **turn-2 Night Spear**; Energy is **10 Darkness** + Dark Patch.
