# How to Pilot — RayEels (2013)

_Format: BW-on 2013 (Plasma Storm/Freeze era). Rayquaza-EX + Eelektrik energy-
acceleration deck._

## One-line identity
A 1HKO machine: **Eelektrik's Dynamotor** moves Lightning from your discard onto the
bench every turn, letting **Rayquaza-EX** fire **Dragon Burst** for 180 — enough to
one-shot almost any EX. Build 3–4 Eels, then take six prizes in three big swings.

## The meta it lived in
2013 was an OHKO arms race. RayEels sat right at the top of it: alongside Black Kyurem
EX, it was one of the few decks that could reliably 1HKO EXs, and it only needed ~4
Energy to do it (versus Darkrai needing a whole LaserBank rig). It "just won't die" —
strong from BLW-DRX all the way through Plasma, and it took a National Championship
in this era. Tropical Beach + Keldeo/Float Stone made its shaky early game livable.

## Engine cards (why they're here)
- **Rayquaza-EX (85/124) ×3** — the attacker. **Dragon Burst**: 180 damage, discard
  all Fire and Lightning attached. That discard is *fine* because Eelektrik just
  refuels it. You need 3 to keep streaming Bursts (a prized Ray really hurts).
- **Eelektrik (40/101) ×4** (from Tynamo ×4) — the engine. **Dynamotor**: once per
  Eel per turn, attach a Lightning from the discard to a benched Pokémon. Three Eels =
  three Energy a turn onto Rayquaza. The whole deck exists to get these online.
- **Keldeo-EX (49/149) ×2** — the streaming trick: after a 180 Burst, Rayquaza has 1
  Energy left. **Rush In** to Keldeo, Dynamotor all the Energy back onto Rayquaza,
  free-retreat with Float Stone → a fully reloaded Rayquaza *every turn* off a single
  copy.
- **Raikou-EX (38/108) ×1** — snipe tech: **Volt Bolt** hits 100 to the bench (KO
  Tynamos/support) and discards Lightning to feed Dynamotor. Great in the mirror.
- **Victini (14/101) ×1** — non-EX attacker (V-create, 100 with full bench) and a
  Fire body to help early Energy / anti-metal.

## Support / tech (why the odd counts)
- **Tropical Beach ×3** — Stadium; draw 7 on slow setup turns. The deck has a weak
  early game, so this is the consistency crutch that makes it tournament-viable.
- **Float Stone ×4** — free retreat for Keldeo/Rayquaza; core to the Rush-In reload
  loop.
- **Ultra Ball ×4 / Level Ball ×4** — get Tynamo/Eelektrik down fast (Level Ball, no
  cost) and discard Lightning (Ultra Ball) to feed Dynamotor. 4-4 Eel line is
  mandatory.
- **Juniper ×4 / N ×4 / Colress ×3** — draw; Juniper discards Energy (good here), N
  disrupts, Colress rewards a wide bench.
- **Pokémon Catcher ×3** — drag an EX up for a 180 KO, or pick off support.
- **Super Rod ×2** — recur Pokémon/Energy so you never run dry of Eels or Lightning.
- **Dowsing Machine (ACE SPEC) ×1** — reclaim a key Item late.
- **8 Lightning / 4 Fire** — Dragon Burst needs both types; Lightning also feeds
  Dynamotor.

## Ideal early game
- **Turn 1–2:** the priority is Tynamo down and Energy in the discard (Ultra Ball,
  Juniper). Get **3–4 Eelektrik** built as fast as possible while surviving.
- **Turn 2+:** once 2–3 Eels are up, Dynamotor + attachment fully charges a benched
  Rayquaza; promote it and Dragon Burst for 180.

## The core loop
Dragon Burst for 180 → Rush In to Keldeo → Dynamotor all the Energy back onto a fresh
Rayquaza → Float Stone free retreat → Burst again next turn. Three Bursts on three EXs
= game. Use Catcher to choose the KO, Raikou to snipe the mirror, Super Rod to refill.

## Priorities / rules of thumb
- **4-4 Eelektrik, 3 Rayquaza** are non-negotiable counts — the deck lives or dies on
  streaming Bursts.
- **Protect Tynamo** — opponents will snipe your 30–40 HP Tynamos to kill the engine;
  Mr. Mime (if teched) blocks that.
- **Get set up, then it snowballs** — a good start wins, a bad start drowns; use
  Tropical Beach to dig out of slow hands.
- Keldeo/Float Stone is your reload — don't forget you can Burst every turn off one
  Rayquaza with it.

## Weaknesses / bad matchups
- **Kyurem PLF** — the nightmare: snipes your Tynamo/Eelektrik and pressures the
  engine. Mr. Mime is the answer.
- **Garbodor (Garbotoxin)** — shuts off Dynamotor; the engine dies.
- **Landorus-EX / fast Big Basics** — early Catcher + snipe on Tynamo before you set
  up; a rough race.
- **Plasma (TDK)** — heavy early pressure and Energy removal (Thundurus's Thunderous
  Noise, Crushing Hammer-style disruption) can starve your Bursts.

## Quick reference — the numbers
- Dragon Burst: **180**, discard all Fire + Lightning on Rayquaza.
- Dynamotor: **1 Lightning from discard → bench**, per Eelektrik, per turn.
- Reload: 180 leaves 1 Energy → **Rush In + Dynamotor** refills to full.
- Core counts: **4-4 Eels, 3 Rayquaza**; Energy **8 Lightning / 4 Fire**.
