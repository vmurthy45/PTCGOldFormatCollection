# How to Pilot — Plasma (2013)

_Format: BW-on 2013 (Plasma Freeze era). "TDK" = **T**hundurus-EX / **D**eoxys-EX /
**K**yurem PLF — regarded as the BDIF (best deck in format) of its time._

## One-line identity
A fast, consistent Team Plasma toolbox: **Thundurus-EX** and **Colress Machine**
accelerate Energy onto a benched **Kyurem**, whose **Blizzard Burn** does big damage as
a *single-prize* attacker, all boosted by **Deoxys-EX's Power Connect**. You spread and
snipe with cheap attacks, then finish with a non-EX hitter — winning the prize trade
by giving up fewer prizes than you take.

## The meta it lived in
After Plasma Freeze, TDK was the deck to beat: fast, consistent, powerful, and full of
1HKO potential. Its non-EX main attacker (Kyurem) meant it traded prizes favorably
against the EX-heavy field, and Colress Machine let it attach multiple Energy a turn.
It stayed dominant even as anti-Plasma tech (Silver Mirror) appeared.

## Engine cards (why they're here)
- **Thundurus-EX (38/116) ×2** — the starter/accelerator. **Raiden Knuckle**: 30
  damage *and* attach any Energy from your discard to a benched Team Plasma Pokémon —
  usually charging a Kyurem. Discard special Energy early (Ultra Ball) to fuel it.
- **Kyurem (31/116) ×4** — the main attacker, and crucially a **non-EX** (1 prize).
  **Frost Spear**: 30 + 30 bench snipe (great opener). **Blizzard Burn**: 120 (can't
  attack next turn) — reset it with a Switch/Float Stone to Burst again.
- **Deoxys-EX (53/116) ×4** — **Power Connect**: boosts your Team Plasma Pokémon's
  damage, stackably. Four benched Deoxys pushes Raiden Knuckle to 70 and Blizzard Burn
  to 160 (before Laser/Bangle). Best sitting on the bench.
- **Colress Machine ×3** — accelerator: attach a Plasma Energy from the *deck* to a
  Plasma Pokémon. It's an Item, so multiples per turn — this is what makes the deck
  truly fast (T1 Frost Spear, T2 Blizzard Burn).
- **Virbank City Gym ×2 + Hypnotoxic Laser ×4** — the damage engine: poison (20 with
  Virbank) + sleep, pushing Kyurem to 1HKO numbers (e.g. Landorus-EX).

## Support / tech (why the odd counts)
- **Team Plasma Ball ×2 / Ultra Ball ×1 / Energy Search ×1** — search; Ultra Ball also
  discards Energy to fuel Raiden Knuckle.
- **Switch ×3 / Float Stone ×2** — reset Kyurem after Blizzard Burn so it can Burst
  again next turn (key to streaming damage).
- **Skyla ×3 / Juniper ×4 / N ×4 / Colress ×2 / Bianca ×1** — the draw/consistency
  spine; Skyla fetches the exact Item (Colress Machine, Laser) you need.
- **Pokémon Catcher ×4** — drag up EXs / support to snipe or KO.
- **Computer Search (ACE SPEC) ×1** — grab any card at the critical moment.
- **Energy: 4 Plasma / 4 Prism / 2 Lightning / 3 Water** — Plasma pairs with Colress
  Machine and powers Deoxys; Prism/Blend cover Kyurem (Water) and Thundurus
  (Lightning). Keeping some basic Water helps against Enhanced Hammer.

## Ideal early game
- **Turn 1:** start **Thundurus-EX**, discard special Energy (Ultra Ball), and Raiden
  Knuckle — 30 damage + an Energy onto a benched Kyurem. Or open Kyurem + Colress
  Machine for an immediate Frost Spear.
- **Turn 2:** with Colress Machine(s) + Raiden Knuckle, a Kyurem is charged →
  **Blizzard Burn** for a big early hit. Get Deoxys on the bench ASAP to boost
  everything.

## The core loop
Charge a benched Kyurem via Raiden Knuckle + Colress Machine, promote it, **Blizzard
Burn**, then Switch/Float-Stone it out and charge the next Kyurem so you Burst again.
Stack **Deoxys** on the bench to raise damage, add **Laser + Virbank** to hit exact
1HKO numbers, and **Catcher** to choose your targets. You give up single prizes off
Kyurem while taking EXs — that prize math wins the game.

## Priorities / rules of thumb
- **Fill the bench with Deoxys** — more Deoxys = higher damage on every attack; 3–4 is
  the goal.
- **Kyurem is a single-prize attacker** — lean on it so the opponent gets fewer prizes
  per KO than you do.
- **Reset Blizzard Burn** with Switch/Float Stone so the "can't attack next turn"
  clause never stalls you.
- **Watch your special-Energy reliance** — Enhanced Hammer and Aegislash-type effects
  punish it; keep some basic Water and don't over-commit Energy to a fragile 130-HP
  Kyurem.

## Weaknesses / bad matchups
- **Enhanced Hammer / special-Energy hate** — the deck leans on Plasma/Prism/Blend;
  stripping it stalls your Bursts.
- **Fighting (Landorus-EX)** — Thundurus is Fighting-Weak; you rely on Kyurem/Deoxys to
  win the trade.
- **Garbodor / Item-lock** — Colress Machine and Laser are Items; shutting them off
  slows the engine badly (and Gothitelle's Magic Room is brutal here).
- **Fragile Kyurem** — 130 HP with a big Energy investment can be punished before it
  Bursts.

## Quick reference — the numbers
- Raiden Knuckle: **30 + attach any Energy from discard to a benched Plasma Pokémon**.
- Blizzard Burn: **120** (can't attack next turn; reset with Switch/Float Stone).
- Power Connect (each Deoxys): stacks damage — 4 Deoxys → Blizzard Burn **160** base.
- Frost Spear: **30 + 30 bench snipe**; add Laser+Virbank (+ Deoxys) for 1HKO lines.
