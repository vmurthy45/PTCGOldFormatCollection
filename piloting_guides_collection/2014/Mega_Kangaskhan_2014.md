# How to Pilot — Mega Kangaskhan (2014)

_Format: NXD-on 2014 (XY "Big Basics + Muscle Band" era). This is the Fairy Box /
"Crazy Punch" archetype — Michikazu Tsuda's build took Top-4 at 2014 Worlds._

## One-line identity
A near-unkillable toolbox: **M Kangaskhan-EX** is a 230-HP wall that punches for big
damage, while **Aromatisse** shuttles Fairy Energy around so you can strip a Pokémon
bare, **Max Potion** it to full with zero downside, and then reload the Energy the same
turn. You out-attrition everything and swap in the right attacker for each matchup.

## The meta it lived in
2014 was a fast Basic-EX format where Muscle Band pushed everyone into high-damage,
one-shot territory. Kangaskhan's answer was to be almost impossible to OHKO (230 HP)
and to erase any chip damage with Aromatisse + Max Potion. It earned Top-4 at Worlds
precisely because the format's OHKO math didn't work against it — though the arrival
of Fighting attackers (Landorus/Lucario) that hit Kangaskhan's Weakness was its
kryptonite.

## Engine cards (why they're here)
- **M Kangaskhan-EX (79/106) ×2** (from Kangaskhan-EX ×2) — the wall/attacker. 230 HP
  makes it a two-shot at best for most decks. **Wham Bam Punch** does big damage on
  double heads (Muscle Band lowers the flips needed to hit 180). It's a Mega, so it
  ends your turn to evolve — plan for that.
- **Aromatisse (93/146) ×2** (from Spritzee ×3) — the engine. **Fairy Transfer**: move
  Fairy Energy between your Pokémon freely. This does two jobs: load an attacker in one
  turn, and pull Energy *off* a Pokémon before Max Potion so you heal without
  discarding Energy.
- **Max Potion ×3** — with Aromatisse, this is a full heal for free: transfer the
  Energy away, Max Potion to full HP, transfer the Energy back. This is the whole
  "unkillable" plan.
- **Fairy Garden ×4** — Stadium giving free retreat to any Pokémon with Energy (trivial
  here). Lets you pivot the right attacker up without paying retreat.

## The toolbox attackers (pick per matchup)
- **Mewtwo-EX (54/99)** — X-Ball; answer to opposing Mewtwo and a splashable heavy hit
  (transfer all your Energy onto it for a huge swing).
- **Xerneas-EX (97/146) / Xerneas (96/146)** — **Geomancy** (Xerneas) is easy Fairy
  acceleration to the bench; Xerneas-EX a Fairy attacker.
- **Keldeo-EX (49/149)** — Rush In pivot / status escape.
- **Suicune (20/101)** — wall vs. EX attackers (takes reduced/again-immunity vs. EX in
  its era text); buys time.

## Support / tech (why the odd counts)
- **Sycamore ×3 / N ×4 / Colress ×3 / Shauna ×1 / Bianca ×1** — the draw spine; N
  disrupts, Colress rewards a wide bench.
- **Ultra Ball ×2 / Level Ball ×2 / Heavy Ball ×1** — search the evolutions and Basics.
- **Muscle Band ×3** — the damage modifier that makes Wham Bam Punch's flips count.
- **Lysandre ×2** — drag up a support Basic or a target you can KO.
- **Tool Scrapper ×2 / Startling Megaphone ×1** — remove opposing Tools; crucial vs.
  Garbodor (which shuts off Fairy Transfer).
- **Super Rod ×1 / Dowsing Machine (ACE SPEC) ×1** — recovery / reclaim a key card.
- **7 Fairy / 1 Water / 1 Rainbow / 2 DCE** — Fairy fuels the engine; Rainbow/DCE
  enable the off-type attackers.

## Ideal early game
- **Turn 1:** bench Kangaskhan-EX + Spritzee; use draw Supporters (Sycamore) to build a
  hand. Start an Aromatisse and put Fairy Energy in play.
- **Turn 2:** evolve into **M Kangaskhan-EX** (this ends your turn) once Aromatisse and
  a couple of Energy are set. From there, punch + Max Potion loop begins.

## The core loop
Attack with M Kangaskhan → next turn, if damaged, **Fairy Transfer the Energy off →
Max Potion to full → Transfer back** and punch again. Because you never actually lose
the Energy, the wall just keeps swinging. Use Fairy Garden free retreat + Lysandre to
line up the KO you want, and rotate Mewtwo/Xerneas/Keldeo in for specific matchups.

## Priorities / rules of thumb
- **Play to the wall, not to speed** — you win by being un-KO-able and healing, not by
  racing. Don't over-extend Energy you can't protect.
- **Sequence the Mega evolve** — it ends your turn, so set up Aromatisse/Energy first.
- **Watch Wham Bam Punch's flips** — Muscle Band lowers how many heads you need; know
  your out before committing.
- **Guard against Garbodor** — Tool Scrapper/Megaphone reinstates Fairy Transfer, which
  is your entire engine.

## Weaknesses / bad matchups
- **Fighting (Landorus-EX / Lucario-EX)** — Kangaskhan is Fighting-Weak; these OHKO
  through your HP and heal plan. The deck's biggest problem after Furious Fists.
- **Garbodor (Garbotoxin)** — turns off Fairy Transfer, breaking the Max Potion combo.
- **Enhanced Hammer / special-Energy hate** — punishes Rainbow-reliant off-type
  attackers.
- **Pyroar** (Intimidating Mane) is *not* a problem — M Kangaskhan isn't a Basic, so it
  KOs Pyroar cleanly.

## Quick reference — the numbers
- M Kangaskhan-EX: **230 HP** (very hard to OHKO).
- Max Potion combo: **full heal, no Energy lost** (via Fairy Transfer).
- Fairy Garden: **free retreat** for Energy-bearing Pokémon.
- Setup: **T2 Mega evolve** (ends turn), then the punch/heal loop.
