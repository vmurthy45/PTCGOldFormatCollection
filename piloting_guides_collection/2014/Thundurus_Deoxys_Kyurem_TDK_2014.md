# How to Pilot — Thundurus/Deoxys/Kyurem (TDK) (2014)

_Format: NXD-on 2014 (XY era). The same Team Plasma "TDK" core as 2013, updated with
Muscle Band and XY trainers — still a top-tier deck into the new season._

## One-line identity
A fast, consistent Team Plasma toolbox: **Thundurus-EX** + **Colress Machine**
accelerate Energy onto a benched **Kyurem**, whose **Blizzard Burn** hits hard as a
*single-prize* attacker, all boosted by **Deoxys-EX's Power Connect**. You spread,
snipe, and 1HKO EXs while giving up fewer prizes than you take.

## The meta it lived in
Carried over from its 2013 dominance, TDK stayed strong in the XY "Big Basics" format.
Muscle Band gave every attack +20, sharpening Kyurem's KO math; Genesect-EX's Red
Signal added a gust effect. Its single-prize main attacker kept the prize trade in its
favor against an EX-saturated field.

## Engine cards (why they're here)
- **Thundurus-EX (38/116) ×2** — starter/accelerator. **Raiden Knuckle**: 30 damage +
  attach any Energy from the discard to a benched Team Plasma Pokémon (usually charging
  Kyurem). Discard special Energy early (Ultra Ball) to fuel it.
- **Kyurem (31/116) ×3** — main attacker, and crucially a **non-EX** (1 prize).
  **Frost Spear**: 30 + 30 bench snipe. **Blizzard Burn**: 120 (can't attack next turn;
  reset with Switch). 1HKOs Landorus-EX with support.
- **Deoxys-EX (53/116) ×4** — **Power Connect**: stackably boosts your Team Plasma
  Pokémon's damage. Four on the bench pushes Raiden Knuckle to 70 and Blizzard Burn to
  160 before modifiers. Best benched.
- **Genesect-EX (11/101) ×1** — **Red Signal**: when you attach a Plasma Energy to it,
  switch the opponent's Active with a benched Pokémon — a gust to drag up your target.
- **Colress Machine ×3** — accelerator: attach Plasma Energy from the deck to a Plasma
  Pokémon. It's an Item, so multiples per turn = the deck's speed.

## Support / tech (why the odd counts)
- **Hypnotoxic Laser ×2 + Virbank City Gym ×1** — poison/sleep to push Kyurem to KO
  numbers.
- **Muscle Band ×4** — +20 per attack; central to Kyurem's 1HKO math in this format.
- **Switch ×3 / Scramble Switch (ACE SPEC) ×1** — reset Kyurem after Blizzard Burn to
  Burst again; Scramble Switch also moves Energy in a pinch.
- **Sycamore ×4 / N ×4 / Colress ×2 / Skyla ×3** — draw + fetch; Skyla grabs the exact
  Item (Colress Machine, Laser) you need.
- **Lysandre ×2** — gust EXs/support up for KOs (complements Red Signal).
- **Team Plasma Ball ×2 / Ultra Ball ×2** — search + Energy discard.
- **Startling Megaphone ×1** — clear opposing Tools (e.g. Garbodor's, or Silver
  Bangle/Muscle Band).
- **Max Potion ×1 / Tropical Beach ×1** — heal a Thundurus (it re-fuels itself); Beach
  digs on slow turns.
- **Energy: 4 Plasma / 4 Prism / 4 Rainbow / 2 Blend WLFM** — heavy special-Energy base
  for Colress Machine + the multi-type attack costs.

## Ideal early game
- **Turn 1:** start **Thundurus-EX**, discard special Energy (Ultra Ball), Raiden
  Knuckle — 30 + an Energy onto a benched Kyurem. Or open Kyurem + Colress Machine for
  an immediate Frost Spear.
- **Turn 2:** with Colress Machine(s) + Raiden Knuckle, a Kyurem is charged →
  **Blizzard Burn**. Get Deoxys down ASAP to boost everything.

## The core loop
Charge a benched Kyurem (Raiden Knuckle + Colress Machine), promote, **Blizzard Burn**,
then **Switch** it out and charge the next Kyurem to Burst again. Stack **Deoxys** for
damage, add **Laser + Virbank + Muscle Band** to hit exact 1HKO numbers, and use
**Red Signal / Lysandre** to pick targets. Single prizes off Kyurem while taking EXs
wins the prize race.

## Priorities / rules of thumb
- **Fill the bench with Deoxys** — 3–4 for maximum damage.
- **Lean on Kyurem's single-prize attacks** to keep the prize trade favorable.
- **Reset Blizzard Burn** every turn with Switch so the "can't attack" clause never
  stalls you.
- **Manage special-Energy reliance** — Enhanced Hammer / Aegislash-type effects punish
  it; don't over-commit Energy to a fragile 130-HP Kyurem.

## Weaknesses / bad matchups
- **Special-Energy hate (Enhanced Hammer, Aegislash-EX)** — stalls the Plasma engine.
- **Fighting (Landorus-EX)** — Thundurus is Fighting-Weak; rely on Kyurem/Deoxys to win
  the trade.
- **Garbodor / Item-lock** — Colress Machine, Laser, Switch are all Items; shutting them
  off cripples the deck (Seismitoad's Quaking Punch is rough).

## Quick reference — the numbers
- Raiden Knuckle: **30 + attach any Energy from discard to a benched Plasma Pokémon**.
- Blizzard Burn: **120** (can't attack next turn; reset with Switch).
- Power Connect (each Deoxys): stacks; 4 Deoxys → Blizzard Burn **160** base.
- Muscle Band: **+20**; Frost Spear **30 + 30 snipe**.
