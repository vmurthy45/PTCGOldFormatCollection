# How to Pilot — Yveltal/Raichu (2014)

_Format: NXD-on 2014 (XY era). A Dark "Big Basics" deck splashing a Raichu line as a
Yveltal-mirror counter._

## One-line identity
One of the two decks that defined 2014: **Yveltal-EX** hits for one of the format's
biggest attacks (**Evil Ball** scales with Energy), **Darkrai-EX** gives the team free
retreat and Dark Patch acceleration, and a **Pikachu/Raichu** line is teched in so the
mirror doesn't come down to a coin-flip — Raichu's **Circle Circuit** 1HKOs opposing
Yveltal-EX.

## The meta it lived in
Yveltal-EX was arguably the most centralizing card of the year — so strong that people
teched Raichu into everything just to beat it (some even inside their own Yveltal
decks, exactly like this list). Muscle Band made every attack hit harder, and the Dark
engine (Sableye, Dark Patch, Yveltal's Oblivion Wing) was the most consistent in the
format. Yveltal-EX went on to headline the metagame for years.

## Engine cards (why they're here)
- **Yveltal-EX (79/146) ×3** — the core. **Evil Ball**: 20 + 20 per Energy on *both*
  Active Pokémon (with Muscle Band + Laser it hits huge). **Y-Cyclone** moves an Energy
  to the bench as you attack — the preferred early attack so you don't overcommit.
- **Darkrai-EX (63/108) ×2** — **Dark Cloak**: free retreat for Darkness-carrying
  Pokémon (mobility for the whole team). A backup attacker and the reason Dark Patch
  works.
- **Pikachu (42/146) ×2 → Raichu (43/146) ×2** — the tech line. **Circle Circuit**:
  10 × your benched Pokémon, for a single DCE. With a full bench it 1HKOs Yveltal-EX
  (and Sigilyph/other Lightning-Weak threats) — your mirror-breaker.
- **Sableye (62/108) ×1** — **Junk Hunt**: recur two Items (Laser, Energy Switch) from
  discard; free retreat under Dark Cloak.

## Support / tech (why the odd counts)
- **Dark Patch ×4** — accelerate Darkness from the discard onto benched Dark Pokémon;
  the reason you can power a big Evil Ball fast.
- **Energy Switch ×2** — move Darkness around to hit exact Evil Ball numbers or fuel a
  quick attacker.
- **Hypnotoxic Laser ×4 + Virbank City Gym ×2** — poison/sleep + damage; pushes Evil
  Ball to KO numbers and disrupts.
- **Muscle Band ×3** — +20; central to the format's KO math.
- **Juniper ×4 / N ×4 / Colress ×2 / Random Receiver ×1** — the draw spine.
- **Ultra Ball ×3 / Level Ball ×1 / Computer Search (ACE SPEC) ×1 / Professor's Letter
  ×1** — search + Energy discard for Dark Patch.
- **Lysandre ×1 / Pokémon Catcher ×1** — drag up targets for KOs.
- **Enhanced Hammer ×1 / Startling Megaphone ×1 / Switch ×1 / Super Rod ×1** — special-
  Energy removal, Tool removal, status escape, recovery.
- **8 Darkness / 4 DCE** — Darkness feeds Dark Patch + Evil Ball; DCE powers Raichu and
  a fast Yveltal.

## Ideal early game
- **Turn 1 (baby Yveltal-style):** going second, use **Oblivion Wing** (baby Yveltal —
  note this list leans on Yveltal-EX/Y-Cyclone) or start chipping and banking Energy in
  the discard for Dark Patch. Bench a Darkrai for free retreat and a Pikachu if the
  mirror looms.
- **Early turns:** **Y-Cyclone** to attack while stashing Energy on the bench, building
  toward a lethal Evil Ball without overexposing Energy to a return KO.

## The core loop
Y-Cyclone early (attack + bank Energy safely) → build a big **Evil Ball** with Dark
Patch + Energy Switch + Muscle Band + Laser for the KO → use Dark Cloak free retreat to
rotate → drop the **Raichu** when you need to 1HKO an opposing Yveltal-EX. Sableye
recurs your Lasers/Items to keep the pressure on.

## Priorities / rules of thumb
- **Prefer Y-Cyclone until the late game** — a huge early Evil Ball just feeds the
  opponent's own Evil Ball/Mewtwo/G-Booster return KO. Build the board first.
- **Bench management for Raichu** — a fuller bench = bigger Circle Circuit; set it up
  before you need the mirror KO.
- **Bank Energy in the discard** for Dark Patch; Juniper/Ultra Ball are your fuel.
- **Laser + Muscle Band** are how a modest Evil Ball becomes lethal — track the numbers.

## Weaknesses / bad matchups
- **Fighting (Landorus-EX/Lucario-EX)** — Darkrai is Fighting-Weak; Yveltal's Fighting
  *resistance* helps, but Fighting decks still pressure the Dark side.
- **Opposing Raichu / Lightning** — your own Yveltal-EX is Lightning-Weak; the mirror
  and Raichu decks punish it (which is why you run Raichu too).
- **Pyroar (Intimidating Mane)** — blocks your Basic attackers; this straight-Dark build
  has trouble (Raichu is a non-answer since Pyroar KOs it).
- **Garbodor** — shuts off Dark Cloak (free retreat) and Dark Patch.

## Quick reference — the numbers
- Evil Ball: **20 + 20 per Energy** on both Active Pokémon (huge with Muscle Band +
  Laser).
- Circle Circuit (Raichu): **10 × your benched Pokémon** for 1 DCE (1HKOs Yveltal-EX on
  a full bench).
- Dark Cloak: **free retreat** for Darkness-carrying Pokémon.
- Y-Cyclone: attack **+ move an Energy to your bench** (the safe early attack).
