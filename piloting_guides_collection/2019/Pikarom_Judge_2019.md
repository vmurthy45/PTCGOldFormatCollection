# How to Pilot — Pikarom Judge (2019)

_Format: Sun & Moon-on Standard 2019 (TEU–UNM). "PikaRom" — the aggressive Lightning Tag
Team deck, here in a disruptive **Judge** build._

## One-line identity
Explosive turn-1 aggression with hand disruption: **Pikachu & Zekrom-GX's Full Blitz**
attacks *and* accelerates 3 Lightning on turn 1, **Electropower** spikes damage for 1HKOs,
and **Judge** keeps resetting the opponent to 4 cards so they can't stabilize. Hit huge,
early, and often while their hand stays small.

## The meta it lived in
PikaRom was the premier Tag Team aggro deck — a turn-1 Full Blitz sets up a turn-2 Tag Bolt-GX
or a huge Fusion Strike-style swing. The **Judge** engine (with Volkner to find your Items)
leans into disruption: keep the opponent's hand at 4 while you power through with a wide
Lightning toolbox (Zeraora-GX, Raichu & Alolan Raichu-GX). It races the Fire decks and
punishes slow setups.

## Engine cards (why they're here)
- **Pikachu & Zekrom-GX (33/181) ×2** — the core. **Full Blitz**: 150 *and* attach 3 Lightning
  from your deck to it — the turn-1 explosion that sets up everything. **Tag Bolt-GX**: 200 +
  170 to a benched Pokémon (the +Energy version) — a double-KO GX attack. Needs to attack the
  turn Full Blitz sets it up.
- **Dedenne-GX (57/214) ×4** — **Dedechange**: discard hand, draw 6 — the draw engine that
  digs to your combo (and a Lightning body). Four copies for consistency.
- **Raichu & Alolan Raichu-GX (54/236) ×2** — **Tandem Shock**: 150 + Paralysis (with extra
  Lightning) — a lockdown attacker that stalls while you set up; a second Tag Team threat.
- **Zeraora-GX (86/214) ×2** — **Thunderclap Zone** (Ability): your Lightning Pokémon with a
  Lightning Energy retreat for free — mobility for the whole team; **Plasma Fists** a solid
  attack and **Full Voltage-GX** for a big hit.
- **Electropower ×4** — Item: +30 damage this turn (stackable) — the 1HKO enabler that turns
  Full Blitz/Tandem Shock into exact KO numbers on Tag Teams.

## Support / tech (why the odd counts)
- **Hoopa (140/236) ×1** — **Scoundrel Guard** (can't be damaged by GX/EX in some lines) / a
  bench attacker; utility body.
- **Marshadow (81/214) ×1** — **Resetting Hole**: discard the Stadium — your out to **Power
  Plant** (which shuts off Zeraora/Dedenne Abilities) and opposing Stadiums.
- **Mew (76/214) ×1** — **Bench Barrier**-style protection: shields your bench from snipe
  (protect Dedenne-GX/Tapu Koko from Tag Bolt/Custom Catcher pings).
- **Tapu Koko Prism Star (51/181) ×1** — **Aeroschnitzel** / its Ability accelerates Lightning;
  a Prism Star accelerator/attacker.
- **Tapu Fini (53/236) ×1** — tech vs. Ultra Beasts / a utility attacker (cheaper vs. UB decks).
- **Judge ×4 / Volkner ×4 / Cynthia ×1** — the Supporter suite. **Judge** disrupts (both hands
  to 4) *and* fixes your own hand; **Volkner** searches a Fire/Lightning Item + an Energy
  (finds Electromagnetic Radar/Electropower).
- **Custom Catcher ×4 / Electromagnetic Radar ×4** — gust (2 at once) and **Radar** (search 2
  Lightning GX for a discard of 2) — the search that finds your Tag Teams fast.
- **Energy Switch ×2 / Tag Switch ×1 / Switch ×1** — move Lightning to the right attacker and
  pivot (dodge gusts, un-paralyze).
- **Reset Stamp ×1 / Stadium Nav ×1** — hand disruption / Stadium search.
- **Lysandre Labs ×2 / Power Plant ×1 / Thunder Mountain Prism Star ×1** — **Lysandre Labs**
  turns off opposing Tools; **Thunder Mountain** reduces your Lightning attack costs
  (cheaper Full Blitz/Tandem Shock); Power Plant shuts off opposing Abilities.
- **11 Lightning** — fuel for Full Blitz's acceleration and attack costs.

## Ideal early game
- **Turn 1 (dream, going first or second):** **Full Blitz** — 150 damage *and* 3 Lightning
  onto Pikachu & Zekrom-GX. You're now set up for a turn-2 Tag Bolt-GX or a big Electropower'd
  swing. **Dedechange** to dig if the hand's rough.
- **Judge** on a turn the opponent is trying to set up — keep them at 4 cards.

## The core loop
Full Blitz turn 1 → **Tag Bolt-GX** / Electropower'd attacks for KOs → **Judge** every time
the opponent tries to rebuild, keeping their hand small. **Zeraora free retreat** lets you
pivot attackers; **Raichu Tandem Shock** paralysis-locks while you reload; **Custom Catcher/
Marshadow** reach the pieces you need. Race to 6 prizes before they stabilize.

## Priorities / rules of thumb
- **Turn-1 Full Blitz** is the whole plan — mulligan your sequencing toward it; the 3-Energy
  acceleration is what makes turn 2 lethal.
- **Electropower math** — know how many Electropower/Choice-band-equivalents you need for the
  1HKO; stacking them is how you break through Tag Team HP.
- **Judge as tempo** — disrupt on *their* setup turns, not yours; it hurts them more when
  they're digging.
- **Marshadow for Power Plant** — your Abilities (Zeraora/Dedenne) matter; clear the Stadium.

## Weaknesses / bad matchups
- **Fighting attackers** — Pikachu & Zekrom-GX is Fighting-Weak (Buzzwole/Lycanroc-type decks
  1HKO it).
- **Single-prize decks (Malamar, Blacephalon)** — trade prizes well against your 3-prize Tag
  Teams; you must race cleanly.
- **Power Plant** — shuts off your Ability engine; find Marshadow.
- **Reset Stamp against you late** — even a disruptive deck can be disrupted off its combo.

## Quick reference — the numbers
- Full Blitz: **150 + attach 3 Lightning from deck** to Pikachu & Zekrom-GX.
- Tag Bolt-GX: **200 + 170 bench** (with extra Energy); Electropower: **+30/use** (stackable).
- Tandem Shock: **150 + Paralysis** (Raichu & Alolan Raichu-GX).
- Judge: **both players draw down to 4**; Thunder Mountain: **-Lightning cost** on attacks.
