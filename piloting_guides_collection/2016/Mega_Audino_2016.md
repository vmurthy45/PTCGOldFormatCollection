# How to Pilot — Mega Audino (2016)

_Format: XY Standard 2016. "Magical Symphony" — Shintaro Ito's 2016 World Championship-
**winning** (Masters) rogue deck, essentially card-for-card._

## One-line identity
A patient control-beatdown: **M Audino-EX** is a 220-HP wall whose **Magical Symphony**
does 110 to the Active *and* 50 to the bench **whenever you play a Supporter** — so your
whole 37-card Trainer engine doubles as attack fuel. You out-tank the format while
sniping fragile benched Pokémon (Shaymin-EX, Joltik) for easy multi-prize turns.

## The meta it lived in
Everyone came to 2016 Worlds prepared for Night March and its counters. Nobody was
ready for Mega Audino — a Japanese rogue whose 110+50 spread was *perfectly* tuned:
110 OHKOs Shaymin-EX and Vespiquen, and the 50 bench snipe simultaneously picks off
Joltik/Combee/Eevee. Its 220 HP + healing made it miserable to KO. It "came out of left
field" and won the Masters Division; ironically, Night March rotated right after, so its
best matchup vanished.

## Engine cards (why they're here)
- **M Audino-EX (85/124) ×3** (from Audino-EX ×4) — the wall/attacker. **Magical
  Symphony**: 110 to the Active + 50 to a benched Pokémon **if you played a Supporter
  this turn**. 220 HP makes it a 3-shot for much of the format. It's a Mega (evolving
  ends your turn) — but Audino Spirit Link fixes that.
- **Audino Spirit Link ×4** — Tool that lets you Mega-evolve Audino **without ending
  your turn**, so you can evolve *and* attack the same turn. Essential.
- **Metal Energy ×6 + DCE ×4** — Metal fuels Magical Symphony's cost and enables
  **Magearna-EX** / **Cobalion**; DCE is flexible.
- **Absol (40/108) ×1** — **Cursed Eyes**: move 3 damage counters on the opponent's side.
  Combined with the 50 bench snipe, it lets you finish off exactly the Pokémon you want
  (Frogadier, Pumpkaboo).

## The toolbox techs (why the 1-ofs)
- **Magearna-EX (75/114) ×1** — **Mystic Heart**: your Metal Pokémon take no effects of
  attacks (other than damage). Shuts off Trevenant BREAK's Silent Fear, Froakie's
  Bubble, and other counter-placement/effect attacks.
- **Cobalion (74/114) ×1** — a Metal attacker; answers **Glaceon-EX / Regice** (which
  block Mega damage) since they're Metal-Weak, and closes games.
- **Hoopa-EX (36/98) ×1** — **Scoundrel Ring**: pull 3 EXs from the deck (Audino-EX +
  Shaymin-EX) for an explosive setup.
- **Shaymin-EX (77/108) ×2** — Set Up burst draw.

## Support / tech (why the counts)
- **Sycamore ×4 / N ×2 / AZ ×2 / Trainers' Mail ×4** — the draw spine. Because *every*
  Supporter triggers Magical Symphony, you want a high Supporter count; **AZ** doubles as
  healing (pick up a damaged Audino) and triggers the attack.
- **Pokémon Center Lady ×1** — heal 60 *and* remove Special Conditions — the key that
  forces opponents to attack Audino a third time, cementing the wall.
- **Parallel City ×2** — versatile Stadium: shrink your *own* bench to dodge snipes, or
  reduce the opponent's bench / their Water damage (great vs. Greninja).
- **Lysandre ×2** — drag up a benched target to KO (or combine with the 50 snipe for a
  double-prize turn).
- **Hex Maniac ×1 / Xerosic ×1 / Startling Megaphone ×1** — Ability lock, Tool/Energy
  removal, Tool removal (answers Garbodor, etc.).
- **VS Seeker ×4 / Ultra Ball ×4 / Mega Turbo ×1 / Float Stone ×2 / Escape Rope ×1 /
  Super Rod ×1** — Supporter recursion, search, fast Energy onto a Mega, free retreat,
  switching, recovery.

## Ideal early game
- **Turn 1–2:** get an **Audino-EX** down (Hoopa-EX can fetch it), attach **Audino Spirit
  Link**, and **Mega-evolve without ending your turn**. Use **Mega Turbo / DCE** to power
  up fast. Then **play a Supporter every turn** so Magical Symphony is always online.

## The core loop
Attack with **Magical Symphony** every turn (always play a Supporter first!) — 110 to the
Active, 50 to a chosen benched Pokémon. Use **Absol** to shift damage so the 50 finishes
a specific target; **Lysandre** to pull a Shaymin-EX up for the double KO; **Pokémon
Center Lady / AZ / Parallel City** to keep Audino un-KO-able. Bring in **Cobalion /
Magearna** for the walls that stop Megas.

## Priorities / rules of thumb
- **Always play a Supporter before attacking** — no Supporter = no Magical Symphony
  bonus. Sequence your turn around it.
- **Aim the 50 snipe deliberately** — it turns single-prize turns into double-prize turns
  (Shaymin-EX on the bench is the dream target).
- **Use Parallel City both ways** — protect your own bench from snipes, or choke the
  opponent's Water damage/bench.
- **Cobalion/Magearna are your answers** to Glaceon/Regice/effect-immunity walls — don't
  forget them.

## Weaknesses / bad matchups
- **Fighting attackers** — Audino is Colorless (some Fighting hits it), and dedicated
  Fighting/Fury decks pressure it.
- **Fast decks that ignore the wall** — a deck that races prizes faster than you can heal
  (though Night March, its main "fast" foe, actually feeds Audino via Shaymin snipes).
- **Ability/effect walls without your techs prized** — if Cobalion or Magearna is
  prized, Glaceon/Regice/Trevenant can be very hard.
- **Garbodor** — turns off Magearna/your Abilities; lean on Hex Maniac/Xerosic.

## Quick reference — the numbers
- Magical Symphony: **110 Active + 50 bench**, *only if you played a Supporter that turn*.
- M Audino-EX: **220 HP** (often a 3-shot).
- Audino Spirit Link: **Mega-evolve without ending your turn**.
- Pokémon Center Lady: **heal 60 + remove Special Conditions**.
